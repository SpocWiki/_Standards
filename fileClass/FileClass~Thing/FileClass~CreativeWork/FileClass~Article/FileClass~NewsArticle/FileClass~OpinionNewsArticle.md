# OpinionNewsArticle
This is a kind of [[FileClass~NewsArticle]]

An [[OpinionNewsArticle]] is a [[NewsArticle]] that primarily expresses opinions rather than journalistic reporting of news and events. For example, a [[NewsArticle]] consisting of a column or [[../../../../../Society/Communication/Media/Creative_Work/Blog]]/[[BlogPosting]] entry in the Opinions section of a news publication.
