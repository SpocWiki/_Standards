## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Carl Bosch (German pronunciation: [kaʁl ˈbɔʃ] ; 27 August 1874 – 26 April 1940) 
> was a German chemist and engineer and Nobel Laureate in Chemistry. 
> He was a pioneer in the field of high-pressure industrial chemistry 
> and founder of IG Farben, at one point the world's largest chemical company.
>
> He also developed the Haber–Bosch process, 
> important for the large-scale synthesis of fertilizers and explosives. 
> It is estimated that one-third of annual global food production 
> uses ammonia from the Haber–Bosch process, 
> and that this supports nearly half of the world's population. 
> In addition, he co-developed the so-called Bosch-Meiser process 
> for the industrial production of urea.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carl%20Bosch)
