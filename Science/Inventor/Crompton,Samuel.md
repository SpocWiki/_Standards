## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Samuel Crompton (3 December 1753 – 26 June 1827) was an English inventor 
> and pioneer of the spinning industry. 
> 
> Building on the work of James Hargreaves and Richard Arkwright, 
> he invented the spinning mule, a machine that revolutionised the industry worldwide.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel%20Crompton)
