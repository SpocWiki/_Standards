## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Louis-Jacques-Mandé Daguerre (də-GAIR; French: [lwi ʒɑk mɑ̃de daɡɛʁ]; 
> 18 November 1787 – 10 July 1851) was a French scientist, artist and photographer 
> recognized for his invention of the eponymous daguerreotype process of photography. 
> 
> He became known as one of the fathers of photography. 
> Though he is most famous for his contributions to photography, 
> he was also an accomplished painter, scenic designer, 
> and a developer of the diorama theatre.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis%20Daguerre)
