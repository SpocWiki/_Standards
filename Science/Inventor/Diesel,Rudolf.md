## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Rudolf Christian Karl Diesel (German: [ˈʁuːdɔlf ˈkʀɪsti̯an kaʁl ˈdiːzl̩] ; 
> 18 March 1858 – 29 September 1913) was a German inventor and mechanical engineer, 
> best known for inventing the Diesel engine, which burns Diesel fuel; both are named after him.
>
