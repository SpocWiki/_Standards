## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Wilson Greatbatch (September 6, 1919 – September 27, 2011) 
> was an American engineer and  pioneering inventor. 
> 
> He held more than 325 patents and was a member of the 
> National Inventors Hall of Fame and a recipient of the Lemelson–MIT Prize 
> and the National Medal of Technology and Innovation (1990).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilson%20Greatbatch)
