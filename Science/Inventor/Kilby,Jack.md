## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Jack St. Clair Kilby (November 8, 1923 – June 20, 2005) was an American electrical engineer 
> who took part, along with Robert Noyce of Fairchild Semiconductor, 
> in the realization of the first integrated circuit while working at Texas Instruments (TI) in 1958. 
> He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics on December 10, 2000.  
>
> Kilby was also the co-inventor of the handheld calculator and the thermal printer, 
> for which he had the patents. 
> He also had patents for seven other inventions.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jack%20Kilby)
