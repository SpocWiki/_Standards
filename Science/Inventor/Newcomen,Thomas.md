## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Thomas Newcomen (February 1664 – 5 August 1729) was an English inventor, 
> creator of the [[../../Technology/Engineering/Mechanical_Engineering/Engine/Steam_Engine/Newcomen_Steam_Engine|Newcomen_Steam_Engine]] in 1712, 
> Baptist preacher by calling and ironmonger by trade.
>
> He was born in Dartmouth, in Devon, England, to a merchant family 
> and baptized at St. Saviour's Church on 28 February 1664. 
> 
> In those days, flooding in coal and tin mines was a major problem. 
> Newcomen was soon engaged in trying to improve 
> ways to pump out the water from such mines. 
> 
> His ironmonger's business specialised in 
> designing, manufacturing and selling tools for the mining industry.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas%20Newcomen)
