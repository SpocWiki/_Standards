## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Scientific Revolution was a series of events 
> that marked the emergence of modern science during the early modern period, 
> when developments in mathematics, physics, astronomy, biology 
> (including human anatomy) and chemistry transformed the **views of society about nature**.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific%20Revolution)
