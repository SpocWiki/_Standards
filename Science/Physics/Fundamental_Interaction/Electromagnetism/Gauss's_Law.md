#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../WikiData/WD~Gauss's_law,173356|WD~Gauss's_law,173356]]

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In electromagnetism, Gauss's law, also known as Gauss's flux theorem 
> or sometimes Gauss's theorem, is one of Maxwell's 4 equations. 
> 
> It is an application of the divergence theorem, 
> and it relates the distribution of electric charge to the resulting electric field.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gauss's%20law)
