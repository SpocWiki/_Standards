## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Condensation** is the change of the [[../../State_of_Matter|state of matter]] from the [[gas]] phase into the [[liquid]] phase, 
> and is the reverse of [[Vaporization]]. 
> 
> The word most often refers to the water cycle. 
> It can also be defined as the change in the state of water vapor to liquid water 
> when in contact with a liquid or solid surface or cloud condensation nuclei within the atmosphere. 
> 
> When the transition happens from the gaseous phase into the solid phase directly, 
> the change is called deposition. 
> 
> Condensation is usually associated with water.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Condensation)
