> Gerd Karl Binnig (German pronunciation: [ˈɡɛʁt ˈbɪnɪç] ; born 20 July 1947) 
> is a German physicist. 
> 
> He is most famous for having won the Nobel Prize in Physics 
> jointly with Heinrich Rohrer in 1986 
> for the invention of the scanning tunneling microscope.
>
