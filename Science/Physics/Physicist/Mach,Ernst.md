## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Ernst Waldfried Josef Wenzel Mach ( MAHK; Austrian German: [ˈɛrnst ˈmax] ; 
> 18 February 1838 – 19 February 1916) was an Austrian physicist and philosopher, 
> who contributed to the understanding of the physics of shock waves. 
> 
> The ratio of the speed of a flow or object to that of sound 
> is named the Mach number in his honour. 
> 
> As a philosopher of science, 
> he was a major influence on logical positivism and American pragmatism. 
> Through his criticism of Isaac Newton's theories of space and time, 
> he foreshadowed Albert Einstein's theory of relativity.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernst%20Mach)
