## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Johannes (Janne)  Robert Rydberg (Swedish: [ˈrŷːdbærj]; 8 November 1854 – 28 December 1919) 
> was a Swedish physicist mainly known for devising the Rydberg formula, in 1888, 
> which is used to describe the wavelengths of light emitted by changes in the energy level 
> of an electron in a hydrogen atom.
>
