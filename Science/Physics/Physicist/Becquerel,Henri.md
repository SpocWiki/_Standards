## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Antoine Henri Becquerel ( bek-uh-REL; French: [ɑ̃twan ɑ̃ʁi bɛkʁɛl]; 15 December 1852 – 25 August 1908) 
> was a French nuclear physicist who shared the 1903 Nobel Prize in Physics 
> with Marie and Pierre Curie for his discovery of radioactivity.
>
