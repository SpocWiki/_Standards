## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> John Dalton(5 or 6 September 1766 – 27 July 1844) was an English chemist, physicist and meteorologist. 
> He introduced the atomic theory into chemistry. 
> 
> He also researched colour blindness; 
> as a result, the umbrella term for red-green congenital colour blindness disorders is Daltonism in several languages.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John%20Dalton)
