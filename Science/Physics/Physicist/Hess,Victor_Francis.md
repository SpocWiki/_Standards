## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Victor Franz Hess (German: [ˈvɪktɔʁ ˈfʁants ˈhɛs]; 24 June 1883 – 17 December 1964) 
> was an Austrian–American experimental physicist 
> who shared the 1936 Nobel Prize in Physics with Carl David Anderson 
> for his discovery of cosmic rays.
>
