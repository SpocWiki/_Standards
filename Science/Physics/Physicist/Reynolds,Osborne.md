# [[Reynolds,Osborne]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Osborne_Reynolds,323267]] 

The dimensionless Reynolds-Number is named after him. 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Osborne Reynolds  (23 August 1842 – 21 February 1912) 
> was an Irish-born British innovator in the understanding of fluid dynamics. 
> 
> Separately, his studies of heat transfer between solids and fluids 
> brought improvements in boiler and condenser design. 
> He spent his entire career at what is now the University of Manchester.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Osborne%20Reynolds)
