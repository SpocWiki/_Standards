## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> An elastic modulus (also known as modulus of elasticity (MOE)) 
> is a quantity that describes an object's or substance's resistance to being deformed elastically 
> (i.e., non-permanently) when a stress is applied to it.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elastic%20modulus)
