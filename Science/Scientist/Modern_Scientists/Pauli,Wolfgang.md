## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Wolfgang Ernst Pauli (German: [ˈvɔlfɡaŋ ˈpaʊli]; 25 April 1900 – 15 December 1958) 
> was a jewish Austrian theoretical physicist and one of the pioneers of quantum physics. 
> 
> In 1945, after having been nominated by Albert Einstein, Pauli received the Nobel Prize in Physics 
> for his "decisive contribution through his discovery of a new law of Nature, 
> the exclusion principle or Pauli principle". 
> The discovery involved spin theory, which is the basis of a theory of the structure of matter.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wolfgang%20Pauli)
