## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Open-source intelligence (**OSINT**) 
> is the collection and analysis of data gathered from open sources 
> (overt sources and publicly available information) 
> to produce actionable intelligence. 
> 
> OSINT is primarily used in national security, law enforcement, 
> and business intelligence functions and is of value to 
> analysts who use non-sensitive intelligence in answering 
> classified, unclassified, or proprietary intelligence requirements 
> across the previous intelligence disciplines.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open-source%20intelligence)
