# [[Protacanthopterygii]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Protacanthopterygii,149091]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Protacanthopterygii** is a superorder of ray-finned fish. 
> They inhabit both marine and freshwater habitats. 
> 
> They appear to have evolved in the Cretaceous or perhaps late Jurassic, 
> originating probably roughly 150 million years ago; 
> 
> fossils of them and the closely related Otocephala 
> are known from throughout the Cretaceous.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Protacanthopterygii)
