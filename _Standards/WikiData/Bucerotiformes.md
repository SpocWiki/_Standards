# [[Bucerotiformes]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Bucerotiformes,20740]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Bucerotiformes**  is an order of birds that contains the 
> hornbills, ground hornbills, hoopoes and wood hoopoes. 
> These birds were previously classified as members of Coraciiformes. 
> The clade is distributed in Africa, Asia, Europe and Melanesia.
>
