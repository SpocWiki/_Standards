## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Glycerol** is a simple [[../Triol]] compound. 
> It is a colorless, odorless, sweet-tasting, viscous liquid. 
> The glycerol backbone is found in lipids known as glycerides. 
> It is also widely used as a sweetener in the food industry and as a humectant in pharmaceutical formulations. 
> Because of its three hydroxyl groups, glycerol is miscible with water and is hygroscopic in nature.
>
> Modern use of the word glycerine (alternatively spelled glycerin) 
> refers to commercial preparations of less than 100% purity, typically 95% glycerol.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glycerol)
