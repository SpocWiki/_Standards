## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Robert Boyle  (25 January 1627 – 31 December 1691) 
> was an Anglo-Irish natural philosopher, chemist, physicist, alchemist and inventor. 
> 
> Boyle is largely regarded today as the first modern chemist, 
> and therefore one of the founders of modern chemistry, 
> and one of the pioneers of modern experimental scientific method. 
>
> He is best known for Boyle's law, which describes the inversely proportional relationship 
> between the absolute pressure and volume of a gas, 
> if the temperature is kept constant within a closed system. 
>
> Among his works, The Sceptical Chymist is seen as a 
> cornerstone book in the field of chemistry. 
> 
> He was a devout and pious Anglican and is noted for his works in theology.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert%20Boyle)
