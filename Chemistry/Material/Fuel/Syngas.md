## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Syngas**, or synthesis gas, 
> is a mixture of hydrogen and carbon monoxide in various ratios.  
> 
> The gas often contains some carbon dioxide and methane.   
> It is principally used for producing ammonia or methanol. 
> 
> Syngas is combustible and can be used as a fuel. 
> Historically, it has been used as a replacement for gasoline 
> when gasoline supply has been limited; 
> for example, wood gas was used to power cars in Europe during WWII 
> (in Germany alone, half a million cars were built or rebuilt to run on wood gas).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syngas)
