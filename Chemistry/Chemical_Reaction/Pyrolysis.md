## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Pyrolysis** (; from Ancient Greek  πῦρ pûr 'fire' and  λύσις lýsis 'separation') 
> is a process involving the separation of covalent bonds in organic matter 
> by thermal decomposition within an inert environment without oxygen.
>
