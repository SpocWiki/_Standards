## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Hydrochloric acid, also known as muriatic acid or spirits of salt, 
> is an aqueous solution of hydrogen chloride (HCl). 
> 
> It is a colorless solution with a distinctive pungent smell. 
> It is classified as a strong acid. 
> 
> It is a component of the gastric acid in the digestive systems of most animal species, including humans. 
> Hydrochloric acid is an important laboratory reagent and industrial chemical.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hydrochloric%20acid)
