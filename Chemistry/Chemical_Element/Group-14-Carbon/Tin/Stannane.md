## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Stannane**  or tin hydride is an inorganic compound with the chemical formula SnH4. 
> 
> It is a colourless gas that ignites on contact with air. 
> SnH4 is a heavy analogue of methane but much less robust.  
> 
> In contrast to this rarely used species, 
> stannane also refers to a large collection of [organotin compound](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Organotin_compound "Organotin compound")s like 
> **Triphenyltin hydride** with the formula (C6H5)3SnH.
