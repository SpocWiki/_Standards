## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Tadeusz Reichstein (20 July 1897 – 1 August 1996), AKA Tadeus Reichstein, 
> was a Polish-Swiss chemist 
> and a Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine laureate (1950), 
> which was awarded for his work on the isolation of cortisone.
