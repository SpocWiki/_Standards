## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> John Cabot (Italian: Giovanni Caboto [dʒoˈvanni kaˈbɔːto]; c. 1450 – c. 1499) was an Italian navigator and explorer. 
> His 1497 voyage to the coast of North America under the commission of Henry VII, King of England 
> is the earliest known European exploration of coastal North America since the Norse visits to Vinland in the eleventh century.
>
> To mark the celebration of the 500th anniversary of Cabot's expedition, 
> both the Canadian and British governments 
> declared Cape Bonavista, Newfoundland as representing Cabot's first landing site. 
> However, alternative locations have also been proposed.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John%20Cabot)
