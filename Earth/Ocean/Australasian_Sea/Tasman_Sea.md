# [[Tasman_Sea]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Tasman_Sea,33254]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Tasman Sea is a marginal sea of the South Pacific Ocean, 
> situated between Australia and New Zealand. 
> 
> It measures about 2,000 km (1,200 mi) across 
> and about 2,800 km (1,700 mi) from north to south. 
> 
> The sea was named after the Dutch explorer Abel Janszoon Tasman, 
> who in 1642 was the first known European to cross it. 
> British explorer Lieutenant James Cook later extensively navigated the Tasman Sea 
> in the 1770s during his three voyages of exploration.
>
> The Māori people of New Zealand call this sea Te Moana-a-Rehua 
> meaning 'the sea of Rehua' which clashes with the Pacific waters named Te Tai-o-Whitirea 
> ('the sea of Whitirea') – after Whitirea, Rehua's lover – 
> at Cape Reinga, the northernmost tip of North Island.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tasman%20Sea)
