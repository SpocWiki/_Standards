## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Bay of Biscay ( BISS-kay, -⁠kee) is a gulf of the northeast Atlantic Ocean located south of the Celtic Sea. 
> It lies along the western coast of France from Point Penmarc'h to the Spanish border, 
> and along the northern coast of Spain, extending westward to Cape Ortegal.
>
> The average depth is 1,744 m (5,722 ft) and the greatest depth is 4,735 m (15,535 ft).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bay%20of%20Biscay)
