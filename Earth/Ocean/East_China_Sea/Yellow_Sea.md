## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Yellow Sea (simplified Chinese: 黄海; traditional Chinese: 黃海; pinyin: Huáng Hǎi; Korean: 황해; Hanja: 黃海; RR: Hwanghae), also known as the North Sea, 
> is a marginal sea of the Western Pacific Ocean located 
> between mainland China and the Korean Peninsula, 
> and can be considered the northwestern part of the East China Sea.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yellow%20Sea)
