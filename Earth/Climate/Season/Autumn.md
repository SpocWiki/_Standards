## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Autumn**, also known as fall (in US and Canada) or harvesttime, 
> is one of the four temperate seasons on Earth. 
> 
> Outside the tropics, autumn marks the transition from summer to winter, 
> in September (Northern Hemisphere) or March (Southern Hemisphere). 
> 
> Autumn is the season when the duration of daylight becomes noticeably shorter 
> and the temperature cools considerably. 
> 
> Day length decreases and night length increases as the season progresses 
> until the winter solstice in December (Northern Hemisphere) and June (Southern Hemisphere). 
> 
> One of its main features in temperate climates 
> is the striking change in colour of the leaves of deciduous trees as they prepare to shed.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Autumn)
