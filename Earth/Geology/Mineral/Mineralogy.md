## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Mineralogy** is a subject of geology specializing in 
> the scientific study of the chemistry, crystal structure, 
> and physical (including optical) properties of minerals and mineralized artifacts. 
> 
> Specific studies within mineralogy include 
> - the processes of mineral origin and formation, 
> - classification of minerals, 
> - their geographical distribution, and 
> - their utilization.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mineralogy)
