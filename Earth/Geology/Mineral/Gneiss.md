# [[Gneiss]]

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Gneiss,166409]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Gneiss** ( NYSE) is a common and widely distributed type of metamorphic rock. 
> It is formed by high-temperature and high-pressure metamorphic processes 
> acting on formations composed of igneous or sedimentary rocks. 
> 
> This rock is formed under pressures ranging from 2 to 15 kbar, sometimes even more, 
> and temperatures over 300 °C (572 °F). 
> 
> Gneiss nearly always shows a banded texture 
> characterized by alternating darker and lighter colored bands 
> and without a distinct cleavage.
>
> Gneisses are common in the ancient crust of continental shields. 
> Some of the oldest rocks on Earth are gneisses, such as the Acasta Gneiss.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gneiss)
