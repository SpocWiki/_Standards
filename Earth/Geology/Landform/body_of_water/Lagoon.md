# [[Lagoon]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Lagoon,187223]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Lagoon** is a shallow body of water separated from a larger body of water 
> by a narrow landform, such as a reef, a barrier island or islands, a barrier peninsula, or an isthmus. 
> 
> Lagoons are commonly divided into coastal lagoons (or barrier lagoons) and atoll lagoons. 
> 
> They have also been identified as occurring on mixed-sand and gravel coastlines. 
> There is an overlap between bodies of water classified as coastal lagoons 
> and bodies of water classified as estuaries. 
> 
> Lagoons are common coastal features around many parts of the world.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lagoon)
