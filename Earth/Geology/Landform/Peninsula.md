## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Peninsula** is a landform that extends from a mainland, 
> is connected to the mainland on only one side, and is mostly surrounded by water. 
> 
> Peninsulas exist on each continent. 
> 
> The largest peninsula in the world is the Arabian Peninsula.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peninsula)
