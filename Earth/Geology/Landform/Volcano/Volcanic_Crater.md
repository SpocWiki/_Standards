## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A volcanic crater is an approximately circular depression in the ground 
> caused by volcanic activity. 
> 
> It is typically a bowl-shaped feature containing one or more vents. 
> During volcanic eruptions, molten magma and volcanic gases 
> rise from an underground magma chamber, through a conduit, 
> until they reach the crater's vent, 
> from where the gases escape into the atmosphere 
> and the magma is erupted as lava. 
> 
> A volcanic crater can be of large dimensions and sometimes of great depth. 
> During certain types of explosive eruptions, 
> a volcano's magma chamber may empty enough for an area above it to subside, 
> forming a type of larger depression known as a [[Caldera|Caldera]]. 
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Volcanic%20crater)
