## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> An **Isthmus** (ISS-məs, ISTH-məs; pl.: isthmuses/isthmi/my; Greek ἰσθμός isthmós 'neck') 
> is a narrow piece of land connecting two larger areas 
> across an expanse of water by which they are otherwise separated. 
> 
> A tombolo is an isthmus that consists of a spit or bar, 
> and a strait is the sea counterpart of an isthmus, 
> a narrow stretch of sea between two landmasses that connects two larger bodies of water.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isthmus)
