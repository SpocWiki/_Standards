## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Zenith** is the imaginary point on the celestial sphere directly "above" a particular location. 
> "Above" means in the vertical direction (plumb line) 
> opposite to the gravity direction at that location (nadir). 
> 
> The zenith is the "highest" point on the celestial sphere. 
> The direction opposite of the zenith is the nadir.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zenith)
