
# [[1695-Great_Famine,Scandinavia+1697]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Great Famine of 1695–1697**, or simply the Great Famine, 
> was a catastrophic famine that affected the present Estonia, Finland, Latvia, Norway and Sweden, 
> all of which belonged to the Swedish Empire with the exception of Norway. 
> The areas worst affected were the Swedish province of Finland and Norrland in Sweden proper.
>
> The Great Famine of 1695–1697 was concurrent with the "seven ill years", 
> a period of national famine in Scotland in the 1690s.
>
