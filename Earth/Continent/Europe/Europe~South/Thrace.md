## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Thrace** (thrayss; Bulgarian: Тракия, romanised: Trakiya; Greek: Θράκη, romanised: Thráki; Turkish: Trakya) 
> is a geographical and historical region in Southeast Europe 
> roughly corresponding to the province of Thrace in the Roman Empire. 
> 
> Bounded by the Balkan Mountains to the north, the Aegean Sea to the south, 
> and the Black Sea to the east, it comprises 
> - present-day southeastern Bulgaria (Northern Thrace), 
> - northeastern Greece (Western Thrace), and 
> - the European part of Turkey (East Thrace). 
> 
> Lands also inhabited by ancient Thracians extended in the north 
> to modern-day Northern Bulgaria and Romania and to the west into Macedonia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thrace)
