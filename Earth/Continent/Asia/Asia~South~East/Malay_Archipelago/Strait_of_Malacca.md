## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Strait of Malacca is a narrow stretch of water, 900 kilometres (560 mi) long 
> and from 65 to 250 km (40 to 155 mi) wide, between the [[../Malay_Peninsula|Malay_Peninsula]] to the northeast 
> and the [[Indonesia|Indonesia]]n island of [[Sumatra]] to the southwest, 
> connecting the Andaman Sea (Indian Ocean) and the South China Sea (Pacific Ocean). 
> 
> As the main shipping channel between the Indian and Pacific oceans, it is one of the most important shipping lanes in the world.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strait%20of%20Malacca)
