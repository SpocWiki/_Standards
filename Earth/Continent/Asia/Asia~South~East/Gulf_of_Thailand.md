#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Gulf_of_Thailand,131217]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Gulf of Thailand (Thai: อ่าวไทย), historically known as the Gulf of Siam (Thai: อ่าวสยาม), 
> is a shallow inlet adjacent to the southwestern South China Sea, 
> bounded between the southwestern shores of the Indochinese Peninsula 
> and the northern half of the Malay Peninsula.  
> 
> It is around 800 km (500 mi) in length and up to 560 km (350 mi) in width, 
> and has a surface area of 320,000 km2 (120,000 sq mi). 
> 
> The gulf is surrounded on the north, west and southwest by the coastlines of Thailand 
> (hence the name), on the northeast by Cambodia 
> and the Mekong Delta region of Vietnam, and opens to the South China Sea in the southeast.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulf%20of%20Thailand)
