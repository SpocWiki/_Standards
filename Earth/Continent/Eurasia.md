## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Eurasia** ( yoor-AY-zhə, also UK:  -⁠shə) is a continent in the Eastern Hemisphere. 
> It is the largest continental landmass on Earth, 
> comprising Europe, Asia, the Arabian subcontinent, and the Indian subcontinent. 
>
> The concept of Europe and Asia as distinct continents dates back to antiquity, 
> but their borders have historically been subject to change. 
> 
> For example, the ancient Greeks originally included Africa in Asia but classified Europe as separate land. 
> Eurasia is connected to Africa at the Suez Canal, 
> and the two are sometimes combined to describe the largest contiguous landmass on Earth, Afro-Eurasia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eurasia)
