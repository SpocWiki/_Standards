## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Paraná River (Portuguese: Rio Paraná [ˈʁi.u paɾaˈna] ; 
> Spanish: Río Paraná [ˈri.o paɾaˈna] ; Guarani: Ysyry Parana) 
> is a river in south-central South America, 
> running through Brazil, Paraguay, and Argentina for some 4,880 kilometres. 
> 
> Among South American rivers, it is second in length only to the Amazon River. 
> It merges with the Paraguay River and then farther downstream 
> with the Uruguay River to form the Río de la Plata 
> and empties into the Atlantic Ocean.
>
> The first European to go up the Paraná River 
> was the Venetian explorer Sebastian Cabot, in 1526, while working for Spain.
>
> A drought hit the river in 2021, causing a 77-year low.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paran%C3%A1%20River)
