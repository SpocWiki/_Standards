## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Lake **Titicaca** (Spanish: Lago Titicaca [ˈlaɣo titiˈkaka]; Quechua: Titiqaqa and Aymara: Titiqaqa) 
> is a large freshwater lake in the Andes mountains on the border of [[../Bolivia|Bolivia]] and [[../Peru|Peru]]. 
> 
> It is often called the highest navigable lake in the world. 
> It has a surface elevation of 3,812 m (12,507 ft).
> 
> Titicaca is the largest lake in South America, both in terms of the volume of water and surface area. 
> It is the 18th largest lake in the world.  
> 
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake%20Titicaca)
