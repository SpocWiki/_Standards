> The **Llanos** (Spanish Los Llanos, "The Plains"; Spanish pronunciation: [los ˈʝanos]) 
> is a vast tropical grassland plain situated to the east of the [[../Andes|Andes]] in [[Colombia|Colombia]] and [[Venezuela|Venezuela]], 
> in northwestern South America. 
> It is an ecoregion of the tropical and subtropical grasslands, savannas, and shrublands biome.
