## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Lake Tanganyika** ( TANG-gən-YEE-kə, -⁠gan-; Kirundi: Ikiyaga ca Tanganyika) is an African Great Lake. 
> It is the second-largest freshwater lake by volume and the second deepest, 
> in both cases after Lake Baikal in Siberia. 
> 
> It is the world's longest freshwater lake. 
> The lake is shared among four countries
> - Tanzania (46%) , 
> - Democratic Republic of the Congo (the DRC 40%), 
> - Burundi, and 
> - Zambia 
> 
> It drains via the Lukuga River into the Congo River system, 
> which ultimately discharges at Banana, Democratic Republic of the Congo into the Atlantic Ocean.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake%20Tanganyika)
