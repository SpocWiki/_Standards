#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Mascarene_Islands,205695]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Mascarene Islands (English: ; French: Mascareignes [maskaʁɛɲ] ) or Mascarenes 
> or Mascarenhas Archipelago is a group of islands in the Indian Ocean 
> east of Madagascar consisting of islands belonging to the Republic of Mauritius 
> as well as the French department of Réunion. 
> 
> Their name derives from the Portuguese navigator Pedro Mascarenhas, 
> who first visited them in April 1512. 
> 
> The islands share a common geological origin beneath the Mascarene Plateau 
> known as the Mauritia microcontinent which was a Precambrian microcontinent situated between India and Madagascar until their separation about 70 million years ago. They form a distinct ecoregion with unique biodiversity and endemism of flora and fauna.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mascarene%20Islands)
