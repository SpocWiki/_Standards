## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In a building or a ship, a **Room** is any enclosed space within a number of walls 
> to which entry is possible only via a door or other dividing structure. 
> 
> The entrance connects it to either a passageway, another room, or the outdoors. 
> The space is typically large enough for several people to move about. 
> The size, fixtures, furnishings, 
> and sometimes placement of the room within the building or ship 
> (or sometimes a train) support the activity to be conducted in it.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Room)
