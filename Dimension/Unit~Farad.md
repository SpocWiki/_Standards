## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Farad** (symbol: F) is the unit of electrical [[../Science/Physics/Electricity/Capacitance|Capacitance]] , 
> the ability of a body to store an electrical charge, in the International System of Units (SI), 
> equivalent to 1 coulomb per volt (C/V). 
> 
> It is named after the English physicist Michael Faraday (1791–1867). 
> In SI base units 1 F = 1 kg−1⋅m−2⋅s4⋅A2.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Farad)
