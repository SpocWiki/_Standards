
# [[Dim~Charge]]  

The SI-Unit for Charge is the [[../Unit/SI-Unit/derived_Unit/Unit~Coulomb]].  
Since 2019 the Charge is defined as exactly 1 Electron Charge every 1.602176634×10[^−19]s .
