
# [[Dim~Frequency]] 

The Frequency is the number of Events within a [[../Dim~Time|Dim~Time]]- Interval. 

It has the same Dimension as the [[../Angular_Dimension/Dim~Angular_Speed]], 
because the Events are often completed Circles of 2*[[Pi]].
