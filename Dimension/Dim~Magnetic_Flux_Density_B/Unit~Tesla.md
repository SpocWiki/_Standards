## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The tesla (symbol: T) is the unit of [[../Dim~Magnetic_Flux_Density_B|Dim~Magnetic_Flux_Density_B]] 
> (also called magnetic B-field strength) in the International System of Units (SI). 
>
> One tesla is equal to one weber per square metre. 
> The unit was announced during the General Conference on Weights and Measures in 1960 
> and is named in honour of Serbian-American electrical and mechanical engineer 
> Nikola Tesla, upon the proposal of the Slovenian electrical engineer France Avčin.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla%20(unit))
