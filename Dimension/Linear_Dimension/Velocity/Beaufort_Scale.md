## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Beaufort scale ( BOH-fərt) is an empirical measure 
> that relates wind speed to observed conditions at sea or on land. 
> 
> Its full name is the Beaufort wind force scale. 
> It was devised in 1805 by Francis Beaufort, a hydrographer in the Royal Navy. 
> It was officially adopted by the Royal Navy and later spread internationally.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beaufort%20scale)
