## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The standard atmosphere (symbol: atm) is a unit of pressure defined as 101325 Pa. 
> It is sometimes used as a reference pressure or standard pressure. 
> It is approximately equal to Earth's average atmospheric pressure at sea level.
>
