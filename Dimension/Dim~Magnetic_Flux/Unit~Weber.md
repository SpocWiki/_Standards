## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In physics, the weber(VAY-, WEH-bər; symbol: Wb) is the unit of magnetic flux 
> in the International System of Units (SI). 
> 
> The unit is derived (through Faraday's law of induction) from the relationship 1 Wb = 1 V⋅s. 
> A magnetic flux density of 1 Wb/m2 (one weber per square metre) is one tesla.
>
> The weber is named after the German physicist [[../../Science/Physics/Physicist/Weber,Wilhelm_Eduard|Wilhelm Eduard Weber]] (1804–1891).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weber%20(unit))
