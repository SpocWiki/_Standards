## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Degree** (in full, a degree of arc, arc degree, or arcdegree), 
> usually denoted by ° (the degree symbol), 
> is a measurement of a plane angle in which one full rotation is 360 degrees.
>
> It is not an SI unit—the SI unit of angular measure is the radian—
> but it is mentioned in the SI brochure as an accepted unit. 
> Because a full rotation equals 2π radians, one degree is equivalent to ⁠π/180⁠ radians.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Degree%20(angle))
