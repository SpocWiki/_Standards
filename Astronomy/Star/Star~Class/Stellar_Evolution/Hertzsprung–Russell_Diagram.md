# [[Hertzsprung–Russell_Diagram]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Hertzsprung–Russell_diagram,3270143]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Hertzsprung–Russell diagram (abbreviated as H–R diagram, HR diagram or HRD) 
> is a scatter plot of stars showing the relationship 
> between the stars' absolute magnitudes or luminosities 
> and their stellar classifications or effective temperatures. 
> 
> The diagram was created independently in 1911 by Ejnar Hertzsprung 
> and by Henry Norris Russell in 1913, 
> and represented a major step towards an understanding of stellar evolution.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hertzsprung%E2%80%93Russell%20diagram)
