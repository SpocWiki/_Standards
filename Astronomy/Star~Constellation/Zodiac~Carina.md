## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Carina ( kə-RY-nə, -⁠REE-) is a constellation in the southern sky. 
> Its name is Latin for the keel of a ship, 
> and it was the southern foundation of the larger constellation of Argo Navis (the ship Argo) 
> until it was divided into three pieces, 
> - the other two being Puppis (the poop deck), 
> - and Vela (the sails of the ship).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carina%20(constellation))
