## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A star system or stellar system is a small number of stars that orbit each other, 
> bound by gravitational attraction. 
> 
> It may sometimes be used to refer to a single star. 
> 
> A large group of stars bound by gravitation 
> is generally called a star cluster or galaxy, 
> although, broadly speaking, they are also star systems. 
> 
> Star systems are not to be confused with planetary systems, 
> which include planets and similar bodies (such as comets).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star%20system)
