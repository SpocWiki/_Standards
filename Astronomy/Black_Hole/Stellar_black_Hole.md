## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A stellar black hole (or stellar-mass black hole) 
> is a black hole formed by the gravitational collapse of a star. 
> 
> They have masses ranging from about 5 to several tens of solar masses. 
> They are the remnants of supernova explosions, 
> which may be observed as a type of gamma ray burst. 
> These black holes are also referred to as collapsars.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stellar%20black%20hole)
