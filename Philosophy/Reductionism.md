## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Reductionism** is any of several related philosophical ideas 
> regarding the associations between phenomena 
> which can be described in terms of simpler or more fundamental phenomena. 
> 
> It is also described as an intellectual and philosophical position 
> that interprets a complex system as the sum of its parts, contrary to holism. 
> 
> Reductionism tends to focus on the small, predictable details of a system 
> and is often associated with various philosophies like 
> emergence, materialism, and determinism.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reductionism)
