## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Worship** is an act of religious devotion usually directed towards a deity or God. 
> 
> For many, worship is not about an emotion, it is more about a recognition of a God. 
> 
> An act of worship may be performed individually, in an informal or formal group, 
> or by a designated leader. Such acts may involve honoring.
>
