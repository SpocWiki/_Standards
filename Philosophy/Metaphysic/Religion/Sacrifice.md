## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Sacrifice** is an act or offering made to a deity. 
> A sacrifice can serve as propitiation, 
> or a sacrifice can be an offering of praise and thanksgiving.
>
> Evidence of ritual animal sacrifice has been seen 
> at least since ancient Hebrews and Greeks, and possibly existed before that. 
> 
> Evidence of ritual human sacrifice can also be found back to 
> at least pre-Columbian civilizations of Mesoamerica as well as in European civilizations. 
> Varieties of ritual non-human sacrifices are practiced by numerous religions today.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sacrifice)
