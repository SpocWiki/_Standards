# [[Baba_Yaga]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../WikiData/WD~Baba_Yaga,187002|WD~Baba_Yaga,187002]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Baba Yaga is a female character (or one of a trio of sisters of the same name) 
> from Slavic folklore who has two contrasting roles. 
> 
> In some narratives, she is described as 
> - a repulsive or ferocious-looking old woman who fries and eats children, 
> - while in others she is depicted as a nice old woman who helps the hero. 
> 
> She is often associated with forest wildlife. 
> Her distinctive traits are flying around in a wooden mortar, wielding a pestle, 
> and dwelling deep in the forest in a hut with chicken legs.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baba%20Yaga)
