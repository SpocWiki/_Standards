#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../WikiData/WD~Dragon,7559|WD~Dragon,7559]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Dragon** is a magical legendary creature that appears in the folklore of multiple cultures worldwide. 
> Beliefs about dragons vary considerably through regions, 
> but dragons in Western cultures since the High Middle Ages 
> have often been depicted as winged, horned, and capable of breathing fire. 
> 
> Dragons in eastern cultures are usually depicted as wingless, 
> four-legged, serpentine creatures with above-average intelligence. 
> 
> Commonalities between dragons' traits are often a hybridization of reptilian, mammalian, and avian features.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dragon)
