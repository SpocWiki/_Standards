# [[Cassiopeia(Mythology)]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Cassiopeia (Ancient Greek: Κασσιόπεια Kassiópeia, Modern Greek: Κασσιόπη Kassiópē) 
> or Cassiepeia (Κασσιέπεια Kassiépeia), a figure in Greek mythology, 
> was Queen of Aethiopia and wife of King Cepheus of Ethiopia. 
> She was arrogant and vain, characteristics that led to her downfall.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cassiopeia%20(mother%20of%20Andromeda))
