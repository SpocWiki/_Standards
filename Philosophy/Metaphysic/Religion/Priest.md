# [[Priest]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Priest,42603]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Priest** is a religious leader authorized to perform the sacred rituals of a religion, 
> especially as a mediatory agent between humans and one or more deities. 
> 
> They also have the authority or power to administer religious rites; 
> in particular, rites of sacrifice to, and propitiation of, a deity or deities. 
> 
> Their office or position is the "priesthood", a term which also may apply to such persons collectively. 
> 
> A priest may have the duty to hear confessions periodically, give marriage counseling, 
> provide prenuptial counseling, give spiritual direction, teach catechism, 
> or visit those confined indoors, such as the sick in hospitals and nursing homes.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Priest)
