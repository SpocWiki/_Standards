## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Philip Melanchthon (born Philipp Schwartzerdt; 16 February 1497 – 19 April 1560) 
> was a German Lutheran reformer, collaborator with Martin Luther, 
> the first systematic theologian of the Protestant Reformation, 
> an intellectual leader of the Lutheran Reformation, and influential designer of educational systems.
>
> He stands next to Luther and John Calvin as a reformer, theologian, 
> and shaper of Protestantism.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philip%20Melanchthon)
