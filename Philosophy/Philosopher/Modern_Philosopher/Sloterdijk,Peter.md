## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Peter Sloterdijk** (; German: [ˈsloːtɐˌdaɪk]; born 26 June 1947) 
> is a German philosopher and cultural theorist. 
> 
> He is a professor of philosophy and media theory 
> at the University of Art and Design Karlsruhe. 
> He co-hosted the German television show Das Philosophische Quartett from 2002 until 2012.
>
