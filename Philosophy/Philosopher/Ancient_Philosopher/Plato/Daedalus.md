## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In Greek mythology, **Daedalus** (UK: , US:  ; Greek: Δαίδαλος; Latin: Daedalus; Etruscan: Taitale) 
> was a skillful architect and craftsman, seen as a symbol of wisdom, knowledge and power. 
> 
> He is the father of Icarus, the uncle of Perdix, and possibly also the father of Iapyx. 
> Among his most famous creations are 
> - the wooden cow for Pasiphaë, 
> - the Labyrinth for King Minos of Crete which imprisoned the Minotaur, and 
> - wings that he and his son Icarus used to attempt to escape Crete. 
> 
> It was during this escape that Icarus did not listen to his father's warnings 
> and flew too close to the Sun; 
> the wax holding his wings together melted and Icarus fell to his death.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daedalus)
