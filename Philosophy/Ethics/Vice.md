## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Vice** is a practice, behaviour, habit or item 
> generally considered morally wrong in the associated society. 
> 
> In more minor usage, vice can refer to a fault, a negative character trait, 
> a defect, an infirmity, or a bad or unhealthy habit. 
> 
> Vices are usually associated with a fault in a person's character or temperament 
> rather than their morality. 
>
> Synonyms for vice include fault, sin, depravity, iniquity, wickedness, and corruption. 
> 
> The antonym of vice is virtue.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vice)
