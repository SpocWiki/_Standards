## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Caribbean Community (abbreviated as CARICOM or CC) is an intergovernmental organisation 
> that is a political and economic union of 15 member states (14 nation-states and one dependency) 
> and five associated members throughout the Americas, the Caribbean and Atlantic Ocean. 
> It has the primary objective to promote economic integration and cooperation among its members, 
> ensure that the benefits of integration are equitably shared, and coordinate foreign policy. 
> The organisation was established in 1973, by its four founding members signing the Treaty of Chaguaramas.
>
> The secretariat headquarters is in Georgetown, Guyana. 
> CARICOM has been granted the official United Nations General Assembly observer status.
>
