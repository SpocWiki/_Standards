## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The International Organization for Migration (IOM) 
> is a United Nations related organization working in the field of migration. 
> The organization implements operational assistance programmes for migrants, 
> including internally displaced persons, refugees, and migrant workers.
>
> The International Organization for Migration is a UN agency based in Geneva. 
> Its director general is Amy E. Pope.
>
