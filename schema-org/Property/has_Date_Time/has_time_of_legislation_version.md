Predicate to describe the date of Legislation.

[is_part_of:: pending:]

Use it like this: 
- [has_time_of_legislation_version::P#Y#M#W#DT#H#M#s.fff] or 
- [ #has_/time_/of_legislation_version::P#Y#M#W#DT#H#M#s.fff] with the [ISO_8601-date Format](../../../ISO/ISO_8601-Date_Time) .

The point-in-time at which the provided description of the legislation is valid (e.g.: when looking at the law on the 2016-04-07 (= dateVersion), I get the consolidation of 2015-04-12 of the "National Insurance Contributions Act 2015")

Formal Predicate: 
[domain::Legislation]
(name::has_time_of_legislation_version)
(range::Date)
