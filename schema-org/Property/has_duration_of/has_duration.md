Predicate to describe the Duration of Audiobook, Episode, Event, MediaObject, 
Movie, MusicRecording, MusicRelease, QuantitativeValueDistribution, Schedule.

- [has_duration::P#Y#M#W#DT#H#M#s.fff] or
