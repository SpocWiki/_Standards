Predicate to describe the Duration of HowTo, HowToDirection.

Use it like this: 
- [has_duration_total::P#Y#M#W#DT#H#M#s.fff] or 
- [ #has_/duration_/total::P#Y#M#W#DT#H#M#s.fff] with the [ISO_8601-Duration Format](../../../ISO/ISO_8601-Date_Time) .
