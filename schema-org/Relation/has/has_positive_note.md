Provides positive considerations regarding something, 
for example product highlights or (alongside [[has_negative_note|negative Notes]]) pro/con lists for reviews.

In the case of a [[../../../Society/Communication/Media/Creative_Work/Review|Review]], the property describes the [[has_review/has_review_item|item Reviewed]] 
from the perspective of the review; 
in the case of a [[../../../Society/Agent/Community/Organization/Business/Product|Product]], the product itself is being described.

The property values can be expressed either as unstructured text 
(repeated as necessary), or if ordered, 
as a list (in which case the most positive is at the beginning of the list).
