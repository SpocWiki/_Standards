A category for the item. 
Categories don't form a controlled Vocabulary; they are often strings. 

Greater signs `>` or slashes `/` can be used to informally indicate a category hierarchy, 
similar to a [[../../../bio/Taxonomy|Taxonomy]].
Categories are less formal than [[../../Class|Class]]es, 
which are extensions to [[../../../Mathematics/Set_Theory/Set(Math)|Set(Math)]]s based on strict Predicates. 
Therefore Categories cannot be used for inference. 

Instead they are used for Navigation and Discovery 
and can be overlapping or even inconsistent,
so no unique Categorization is possible.
