## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Recreation** is an activity of leisure, leisure being discretionary time. 
> The "need to do something for recreation" 
> is an essential element of human biology and psychology. 
> 
> Recreational activities are often done for enjoyment, amusement, or pleasure 
> and are considered to be "fun".
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Recreation)
