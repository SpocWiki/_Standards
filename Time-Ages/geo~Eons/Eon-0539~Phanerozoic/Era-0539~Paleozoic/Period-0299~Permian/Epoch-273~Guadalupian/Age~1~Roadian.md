## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In the geologic timescale, the **Roadian** is an age or stage of the Permian. It is the earliest or lower of three subdivisions of the Guadalupian Epoch or Series. The Roadian lasted between 273.01 and 266.9 million years ago (Ma). It was preceded by the [[../Epoch-298~Cisuralian/Age~4~Kungurian|Age~4~Kungurian]] and followed by the [[Age~2~Wordian]].
>
