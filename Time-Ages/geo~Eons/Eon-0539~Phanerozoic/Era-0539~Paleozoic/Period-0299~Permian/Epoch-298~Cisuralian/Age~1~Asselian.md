## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In the geologic timescale, the **Asselian** is the earliest geochronologic age 
> or lowermost chronostratigraphic stage of the Permian. 
> 
> It is a subdivision of the Cisuralian Epoch or Series. 
> 
> The Asselian lasted between 298.9 and 293.52 million years ago (Ma). 
> It was preceded by the Gzhelian (the latest or uppermost subdivision in the Carboniferous) 
> and followed by the [[Age~2~Sakmarian]].
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asselian)
