# [[0744-Uyghur_Khaganate+0840]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Uyghur_Khaganate,831218|WD~Uyghur_Khaganate,831218]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Uyghur Khaganate, Khanate, or Empire, self defined as the Toquz Oghuz ("Nine Tribes") 
> and known to the Tang Chinese as the Jiuxing  
> was a Turkic empire that existed for about a century between the mid 8th and 9th centuries. 
> It was a tribal confederation under the Uyghur nobility.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uyghur%20Khaganate)
