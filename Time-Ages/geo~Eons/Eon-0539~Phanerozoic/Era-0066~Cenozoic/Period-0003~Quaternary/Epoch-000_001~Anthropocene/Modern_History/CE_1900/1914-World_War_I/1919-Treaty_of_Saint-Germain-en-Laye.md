# [[1919-Treaty_of_Saint-Germain-en-Laye]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Treaty_of_Saint-Germain-en-Laye,192924]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Treaty of Saint-Germain-en-Laye (French: Traité de Saint-Germain-en-Laye) 
> was signed on 10 September 1919 by the victorious Allies of World War I on the one hand 
> and by the Republic of German-Austria on the other. 
> 
> Like the Treaty of Trianon with Hungary 
> and the Treaty of Versailles with the Weimar Republic, 
> it contained the Covenant of the League of Nations 
> and as a result was not ratified by the United States 
> but was followed by the US–Austrian Peace Treaty of 1921.
>
> The treaty signing ceremony took place at the Château de Saint-Germain-en-Laye.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty%20of%20Saint-Germain-en-Laye%20(1919))
