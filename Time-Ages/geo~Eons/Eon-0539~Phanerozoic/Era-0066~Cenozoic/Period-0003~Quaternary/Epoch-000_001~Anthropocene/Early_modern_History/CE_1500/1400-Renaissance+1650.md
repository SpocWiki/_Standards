## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Renaissance** (UK:  rin-AY-sənss, US:   REN-ə-sahnss) 
> is a period of history and a European cultural movement covering the 15th and 16th centuries. 
> It marked the transition from the Middle Ages to modernity 
> and was characterized by an effort to revive 
> and surpass the ideas and achievements of classical antiquity. 
> 
> Associated with great social change in most fields and disciplines, 
> including art, architecture, politics, literature, exploration and science,

> then spread to the rest of Italy and later throughout Europe.

> while the corresponding French word renaissance was adopted into English

> in particular with the writings of Dante and the paintings of Giotto.
>

> the development of linear perspective

> and gradual but widespread educational reform.

>

>
