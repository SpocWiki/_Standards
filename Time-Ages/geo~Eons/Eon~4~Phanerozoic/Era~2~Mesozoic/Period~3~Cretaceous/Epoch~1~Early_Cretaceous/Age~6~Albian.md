## #has_/text_of_/abstract 


> The **Albian** is both an age of the geologic timescale and a stage in the stratigraphic column. It is the youngest or uppermost subdivision of the Early/Lower Cretaceous Epoch/Series. Its approximate time range is 113.0 ± 1.0 Ma to 100.5 ± 0.9 Ma (million years ago). The Albian is preceded by the [[Age~5~Aptian]] and followed by the [[../Epoch~2~Late_Cretaceous/Age~1~Cenomanian|Age~1~Cenomanian]].
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albian)
