# [[Cauchy_Product]] 

The Product of 2 Sums is calculated as the Sum of diagonal Products to ensure convergence. 

${\displaystyle \left(\sum _{i=0}^{\infty }a_{i}\right)\cdot \left(\sum _{j=0}^{\infty }b_{j}\right)=\sum _{k=0}^{\infty }c_{k}}$
with ${c_{k}=\sum _{l=0}^{k}a_{l}b_{k-l}}$ .

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In mathematics, more specifically in mathematical analysis, 
> the **Cauchy product** is the discrete convolution of two infinite series. 
> 
> It is named after the French mathematician Augustin-Louis Cauchy.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cauchy%20product)
