## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In mathematics, a limit is the value that a function (or sequence) approaches 
> as the argument (or index) approaches some value. 
> 
> Limits of functions are essential to calculus and mathematical analysis, 
> and are used to define continuity, derivatives, and integrals.
>
> The concept of a limit of a sequence 
> is further generalized to the concept of a limit of a topological net, 
> and is closely related to limit and direct limit in category theory.
>
> The limit inferior and limit superior provide generalizations of the concept of a limit 
> which are particularly relevant when the limit at a point may not exist.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Limit%20(mathematics))
