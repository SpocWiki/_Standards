## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In geometry, a **Hexagon** (from Greek ἕξ, hex, meaning "six", and γωνία, gonía, meaning "corner, angle") 
> is a six-sided polygon. 
> 
> The total of the internal angles of any simple (non-self-intersecting) hexagon is 720°.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hexagon)
