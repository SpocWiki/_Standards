# [[Pentagon]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Pentagon,127840]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In geometry, a **Pentagon** (from Greek  πέντε (pente) 'five' and  γωνία (gonia) 'angle') 
> is any five-sided polygon or 5-gon.  
> The sum of the internal angles in a simple pentagon is 540°.
>
> A pentagon may be simple or self-intersecting. 
> A self-intersecting regular pentagon (or star pentagon) is called a pentagram.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pentagon)
