#is_/same_as :: [[../../../WikiData/WD~Rhombus,41159|WD~Rhombus,41159]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In geometry, a **Rhombus** (pl.: rhombi or rhombuses) is an equilateral quadrilateral, 
> a quadrilateral whose four sides all have the same length. 
> Other names for rhombus include diamond, lozenge, and calisson.
>
> Every rhombus is a simple polygon (having no self-intersections). 
> A rhombus is a special case of a parallelogram and a kite. 
> A rhombus with right angles is a square. 
> 
> A non-square rhombus has two opposite acute angles and two opposite obtuse angles.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rhombus)
