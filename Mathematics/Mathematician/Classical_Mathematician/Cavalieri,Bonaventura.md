## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Bonaventura Francesco Cavalieri (Latin: Bonaventura Cavalerius; 1598 – 30 November 1647) 
> was an Italian mathematician and a Jesuate. 
> He is known for his work on the problems of optics and motion, work on indivisibles, 
> the precursors of infinitesimal calculus, and the introduction of logarithms to Italy. 
> 
> Cavalieri's principle in geometry partially anticipated integral calculus.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bonaventura%20Cavalieri)
