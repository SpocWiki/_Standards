## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Johann Peter Gustav Lejeune Dirichlet (German: [ləˈʒœn diʁiˈkleː]; 13 February 1805 – 5 May 1859) 
> was a German mathematician. 
> 
> In number theory, he proved special cases of Fermat's Last Theorem 
> and created analytic number theory. 
> 
> In analysis, he advanced the theory of Fourier series 
> and was one of the first to give the modern formal definition of a function. 
> 
> In mathematical physics, he studied potential theory, boundary-value problems, 
> and heat diffusion, and hydrodynamics.
>
> Although his surname is Lejeune Dirichlet, he is commonly referred to by his mononym Dirichlet, in particular for results named after him.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter%20Gustav%20Lejeune%20Dirichlet)
