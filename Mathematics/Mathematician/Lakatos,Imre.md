## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Imre Lakatos (UK: , US: ; Hungarian: Lakatos Imre [ˈlɒkɒtoʃ ˈimrɛ]; 9 November 1922 – 2 February 1974) was a Hungarian philosopher of mathematics and science, 
> known for his thesis of the fallibility of mathematics 
> and its "methodology of proofs and refutations" in its pre-axiomatic stages of development, 
> and also for introducing the concept of the "research programme" in his methodology of scientific research programmes.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imre%20Lakatos)
