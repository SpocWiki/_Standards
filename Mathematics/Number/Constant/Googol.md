## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Googol** is the large number 10^100 or ten to the power of one hundred. 
> In decimal notation, it is written as the digit 1 followed by one hundred zeros: 10,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000. 
> 
> Its systematic name is ten duotrigintillion (short scale) or ten sexdecilliard (long scale). 
> Its prime factorization is 2^100 × 5^100.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Googol)
