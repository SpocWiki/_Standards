## Open_WebUI

Admins of a Workspace can configure the WebUI with :  
- System Prompt
- Advanced Params
- Prompts 
- Knowledge-Collections with Upload 
- Tools to select toolkits 
- Skills (add them to the "Skills" workspace first).
