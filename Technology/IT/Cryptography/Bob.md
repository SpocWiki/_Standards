## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Bob is a male given name or a hypocorism, usually of Robert; 
> and sometimes a diminutive of Bobby.
>
> The name most likely originated from the hypocorism Rob, short for Robert. 
> Rhyming names were popular in the Middle Ages, so 
> - Richard became Rick, Hick, or Dick, 
> - William became Will, Gill, or Bill, and 
> - Robert became Rob, Hob, Dob, Nob, or Bob.
>
> The name Bob has shrunken in popularity in the past 60 years. 
> In 1960, nearly 3,000 babies in the United States were given the name Bob, 
> compared to fewer than 50 in 2000.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bob%20(given%20name))
