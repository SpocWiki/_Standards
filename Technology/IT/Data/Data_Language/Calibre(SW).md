## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Calibre ( KAL-ih-bər) is a cross-platform free and open-source suite of e-book software. 
> Calibre supports organizing existing e-books into virtual libraries, 
> displaying, editing, creating and converting e-books, 
> as well as syncing e-books with a variety of e-readers. 
> 
> Calibre has a large collection of community contributed plugins.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calibre%20(software))
