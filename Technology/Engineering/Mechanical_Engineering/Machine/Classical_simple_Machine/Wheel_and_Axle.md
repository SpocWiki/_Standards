# [[Wheel_and_Axle]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../WikiData/WD~Wheel_and_axle,592289|WD~Wheel_and_axle,592289]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The wheel and axle is a simple machine, 
> consisting of a wheel attached to a smaller axle so that these two parts rotate together, 
> in which a force is transferred from one to the other. 
> 
> The wheel and axle can be viewed as a version of the lever, 
> with a drive force applied tangentially to the perimeter of the wheel, 
> and a load force applied to the axle supported in a bearing, which serves as a fulcrum.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wheel%20and%20axle)
