# [[Pulley]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../WikiData/WD~Pulley,48378|WD~Pulley,48378]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Pulley** is a wheel on an axle or shaft 
> enabling a taut cable or belt passing over the wheel to **move and change direction**, 
> or transfer power between itself and a shaft.
>
> A pulley may have a groove or grooves between flanges around its circumference 
> to locate the cable or belt. 
> 
> The drive element of a pulley system can be a rope, cable, belt, or chain.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pulley)
