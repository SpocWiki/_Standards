## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Scarf** (pl.: scarves or scarfs) is a long piece of fabric 
> that is worn on or around the neck, shoulders, or head. 
> 
> A scarf is used for warmth, sun protection, cleanliness, fashion, religious reasons, 
> or to show support for a sports club or team. 
> 
> Scarves can be made from materials including wool, linen, silk, and cotton. 
> It is a common type of neckwear and a perennial accessory.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scarf)
