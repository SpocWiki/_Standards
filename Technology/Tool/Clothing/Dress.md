## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Dress** (also known as a frock or a gown) is a one-piece outer garment 
> that is worn on the torso, hangs down over the legs. 
> Dresses often consist of a bodice attached to a skirt. 
>
> Dress shapes, silhouettes, textiles, and colors vary. 
> In particular, dresses can vary by sleeve length, neckline, skirt length, or hemline. 
> These variances may be based on considerations such as fashion trends, modesty, weather, and personal taste. 
> Dresses are generally suitable for both formal wear and casual wear in the West.
>
