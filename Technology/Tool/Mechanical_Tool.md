
## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Hand tool** is any tool that is powered by hand rather than a motor. 
> Categories of hand tools include wrenches, pliers, cutters, files, striking tools, 
> struck or hammered tools, screwdrivers, vises, clamps, snips, hacksaws, drills, and knives.
>
> Outdoor tools such as garden forks, pruning shears, and rakes are additional forms of hand tools. Portable power tools are not hand tools.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hand%20tool)
