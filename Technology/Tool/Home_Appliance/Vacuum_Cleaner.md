## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A vacuum cleaner, also known simply as a vacuum (or a hoover in the UK), 
> is a device that uses suction, and often agitation, in order to remove dirt and other debris 
> from carpets, hard floors, and other surfaces.
>
> The dirt is collected into a dust bag or a plastic bin. 
> Vacuum cleaners, which are used in homes as well as in commercial settings, 
> exist in a variety of sizes and types, including stick vacuums, 
> handheld vacuums, upright vacuums, and canister vacuums. 
> 
> Specialized shop vacuums can be used to clean both solid debris and liquids.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vacuum%20cleaner)
