## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A washing machine (laundry machine, clothes washer, or washer) 
> is a machine designed to launder clothing. 
> 
> The term is mostly applied to machines that use water. 
> Other ways of doing laundry include dry cleaning 
> (which uses alternative cleaning fluids and is performed by specialist businesses) 
> and ultrasonic cleaning.
>
> Modern-day home appliances use electric power to automatically clean clothes. 
> The user adds laundry detergent, 
> which is sold in liquid, powder, or dehydrated sheet form, to the wash water. 
> 
> The machines are also found in commercial laundromats where customers pay-per-use.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washing%20machine) 
>
