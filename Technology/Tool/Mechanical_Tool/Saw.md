## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Saw** is a tool consisting of a tough blade, wire, or chain 
> with a hard toothed edge used to cut through material. 
> 
> Various terms are used to describe toothed and abrasive saws.
>
> Saws began as serrated materials, and when mankind learned how to use iron, 
> it became the preferred material for saw blades of all kinds. 
> 
> There are numerous types of hand saws and mechanical saws, 
> and different types of blades and cuts.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saw)
