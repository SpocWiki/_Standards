## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Rope** is a group of yarns, plies, fibres, or strands 
> that are twisted or braided together into a larger and stronger form. 
> 
> Ropes have high tensile strength and can be used for dragging and lifting. 
> Rope is thicker and stronger than similarly constructed cord, string, and twine.
>
