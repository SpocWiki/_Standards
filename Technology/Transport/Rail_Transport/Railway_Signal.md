## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A railway signal is a visual display device that conveys instructions 
> or provides warning regarding the driver's authority to proceed. 
> 
> The driver interprets the signal's indication and acts accordingly. 
> Typically, a signal might inform the driver of the speed 
> at which the train may safely proceed or it may instruct the driver to stop.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Railway%20signal)
