## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Travel** is the movement of people between distant geographical locations. 
> 
> Travel can be done by foot, bicycle, automobile, train, boat, bus, airplane, ship 
> or other means, with or without luggage, and can be one way or round trip. 
> 
> Travel can also include relatively short stays between successive movements, 
> as in the case of tourism.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travel)
