## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Stefan Sagmeister** (born August 6, 1962) 
> is an Austrian graphic designer, storyteller, and typographer based in New York City. 
> In 1993, Sagmeister founded his company, Sagmeister Inc., to create designs for the music industry. 
> He has designed album covers for 
> - Lou Reed, 
> - OK Go, 
> - The Rolling Stones, 
> - David Byrne, 
> - Jay Z, 
> - Aerosmith, 
> - Talking Heads, 
> - Brian Eno and 
> - Pat Metheny. 
> 
> From 2011 until 2019 he partnered with Jessica Walsh under the name Sagmeister & Walsh Inc.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stefan%20Sagmeister)
