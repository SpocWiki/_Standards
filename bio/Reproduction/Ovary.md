# [[Ovary]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Ovary,9631]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Ovary** (from Latin  ōvārium 'egg') 
> is a gonad in the female reproductive system that produces ova; 
> when released, an ovum travels through the fallopian tube/oviduct into the uterus. 
> 
> There is an ovary on the left and the right side of the body. 
> The ovaries are endocrine glands, secreting various hormones 
> that play a role in the menstrual cycle and fertility. 
> 
> The ovary progresses through many stages beginning in the prenatal period through menopause.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ovary)
