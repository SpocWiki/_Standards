# [[External_Parasite]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> An **Ectoparasitic infestation** is a parasitic disease 
> caused by organisms that live primarily on the surface of the host.
>
> Examples:
> - [[../../Medicine/Medical_Condition/Disease/Infectious_Disease/Scabies|Scabies]]
> - Crab [[../../bio~Domain/Eukarya/Animal/Bilateria/Arthropoda/Hexapoda/Insect/Pterygota/Neoptera/Hemipteroid/Psocodea/Troctomorpha/Louse|louse]] (pubic lice)
> - Pediculosis (head lice)
> - Gamasoidosis (avian mites)
> - Lernaeocera branchialis (cod worm)
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ectoparasitic%20infestation)
