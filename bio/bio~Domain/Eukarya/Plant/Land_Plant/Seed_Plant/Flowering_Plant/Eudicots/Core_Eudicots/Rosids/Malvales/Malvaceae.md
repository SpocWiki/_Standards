# [[Malvaceae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Malvaceae,156551]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Malvaceae**, or the mallows, is a family of flowering plants 
> estimated to contain 244 genera with 4225 known species. 
> 
> Well-known members of economic importance include 
> cacao, cola, cotton, okra, roselle and durian. 
> 
> There are also some genera containing familiar ornamentals, such as 
> Alcea (hollyhock), Malva (mallow), and Tilia (lime or linden tree). The genera with the largest numbers of species include Hibiscus (434 species), Pavonia (291 species), Sida (275 species), Ayenia (216 species), Dombeya (197 species), and Sterculia (181 species).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malvaceae)
