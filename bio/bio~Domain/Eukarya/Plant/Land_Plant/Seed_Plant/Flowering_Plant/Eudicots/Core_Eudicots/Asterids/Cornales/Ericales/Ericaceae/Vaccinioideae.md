
# [[Vaccinioideae]] 

#is_/same_as :: 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Vaccinioideae** is a flowering-plant subfamily in the family Ericaceae. It contains the commercially important cranberry, blueberry, bilberry, lingonberry, and huckleberry.
