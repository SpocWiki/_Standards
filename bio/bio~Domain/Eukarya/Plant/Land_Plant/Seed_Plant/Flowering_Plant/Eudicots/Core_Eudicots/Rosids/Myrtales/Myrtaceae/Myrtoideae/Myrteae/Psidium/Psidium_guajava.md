## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Psidium guajava**, the common guava, yellow guava, lemon guava, or apple guava 
> is an evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Americas. 
> It is pollinated by insects. 
> When cultivated, it is pollinated mainly by the common honey bee, Apis mellifera.
>
