#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Byttnerioideae,652608]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Byttnerioideae** is a subfamily of the flowering plant family Malvaceae.
>
> Byttneria is the type genus for the subfamily. 
> Byttneria is now treated as synonym of Ayenia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Byttnerioideae)
