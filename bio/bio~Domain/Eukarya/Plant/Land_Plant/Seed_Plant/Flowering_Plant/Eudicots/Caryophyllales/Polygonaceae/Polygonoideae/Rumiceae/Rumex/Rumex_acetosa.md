# [[Rumex_acetosa]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Rumex_acetosa,26297]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Sorrel (Rumex acetosa), also called common sorrel or garden sorrel, 
> is a perennial herbaceous plant in the family Polygonaceae. 
> 
> Other names for sorrel include spinach dock and narrow-leaved dock 
> ("dock" being a common name for the genus Rumex).
>
> Sorrel is native to Eurasia and a common plant in grassland habitats. 
> It is often cultivated as a leaf vegetable or herb.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sorrel)
