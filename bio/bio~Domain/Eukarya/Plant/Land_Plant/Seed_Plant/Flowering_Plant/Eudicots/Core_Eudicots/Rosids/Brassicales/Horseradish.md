# [[Horseradish]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Horseradish_plant,26545|WD~Horseradish_plant,26545]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Horseradish** (Armoracia rusticana, syn. Cochlearia armoracia) is a perennial plant 
> of the family Brassicaceae (which also includes mustard, wasabi, broccoli, cabbage, and radish). 
> 
> It is a root vegetable, cultivated and used worldwide as a spice and as a condiment. 
> The species is probably native to Southeastern Europe and Western Asia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horseradish)
