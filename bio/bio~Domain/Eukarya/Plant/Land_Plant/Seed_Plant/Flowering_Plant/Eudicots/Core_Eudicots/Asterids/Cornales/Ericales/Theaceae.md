## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Theaceae**, the tea family, is a family of flowering plants comprising shrubs and trees, 
> including the economically important tea plant, and the ornamental camellias. 
> 
> It can be described as having from seven to 40 genera, 
> depending on the source and the method of circumscription used. 
> 
> The family Ternstroemiaceae has been included within Theaceae; 
> however, the APG III system of 2009 places it instead in Pentaphylacaceae. 
> Most but not all species are native to China and East Asia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theaceae)
