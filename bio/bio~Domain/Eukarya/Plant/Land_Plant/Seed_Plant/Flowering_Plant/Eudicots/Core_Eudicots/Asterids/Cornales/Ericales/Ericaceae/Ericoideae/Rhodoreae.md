# [[Rhodoreae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Rhodoreae,15839119|WD~Rhodoreae,15839119]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Rhodoreae** is a tribe of flowering plants in the subfamily of Ericoideae 
> in the family of Ericaceae. 
> 
> It contains one genus, Rhododendron, 
> members of which are known as the rhododendrons or azaleas. 
> 
> It formerly contained Menziesia, now a section of the subgenus Azaleastrum.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rhodoreae)
