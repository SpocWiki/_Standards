# [[Convolvulaceae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Convolvulaceae,145777]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Convolvulaceae**, commonly called the bindweeds or morning glories, 
> is a family of about 60 genera and more than 1,650 species. 
> 
> These species are primarily herbaceous vines, but also include trees, shrubs and herbs. 
> The tubers of several species are edible, the best known of which is the sweet potato.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Convolvulaceae)
