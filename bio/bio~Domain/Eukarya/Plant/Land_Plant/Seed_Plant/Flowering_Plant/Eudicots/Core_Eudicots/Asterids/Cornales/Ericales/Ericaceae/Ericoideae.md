# [[Ericoideae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Ericoideae,933098|WD~Ericoideae,933098]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Ericoideae** is a subfamily of Ericaceae, containing nineteen genera, 
> and 1,790 species, the largest of which is Rhododendron, followed by Erica. 
> 
> The Ericoideae bear spiral leaves with flat laminae. 
> The pedicel is articulated and the flowers are pendulous or erect, 
> and monosymmetric, with an abaxial median sepal. 
> 
> The carpels are free and the anthers lack appendages. The capsule is septicidal.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ericoideae)
