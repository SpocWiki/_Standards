# [[Rapeseed]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Brassica_napus,177932]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Rapeseed (Brassica napus subsp. napus), also known as rape and oilseed rape and canola, 
> is a bright-yellow flowering member of the family Brassicaceae (mustard / cabbage family), 
> cultivated mainly for its oil-rich seed, 
> which naturally contains appreciable amounts of mildly toxic erucic acid. 
> 
> The term "canola" denotes a group of rapeseed cultivars 
> that were bred to have very low levels of erucic acid 
> and which are especially prized for use as human and animal food. 
> 
> Rapeseed is the third-largest source of vegetable oil 
> and the second-largest source of protein meal in the world.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapeseed)
