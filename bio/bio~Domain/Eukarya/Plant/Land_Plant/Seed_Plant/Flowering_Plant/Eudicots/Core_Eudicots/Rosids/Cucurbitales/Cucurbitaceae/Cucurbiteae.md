# [[Cucurbiteae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Cucurbiteae,5192459]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Cucurbiteae** are a tribe of the subfamily Cucurbitoideae, 
> which is part of the flowering plant family Cucurbitaceae (gourds). 
> 
> Species are usually monoecious herbaceous annuals or woody lianas.
>
> The tribe consists of 13 genera reported, but a 2011 study based on genetics reported 11. Members of the genus Cucurbita produce economically valuable fruits, namely squashes and pumpkins.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cucurbiteae)
