## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Crocus sativus**, commonly known as saffron crocus or autumn crocus, 
> is a species of flowering plant in the iris family Iridaceae. 
> 
> A cormous autumn-flowering cultivated perennial, unknown in the wild, 
> it is best known for the culinary use of its floral stigmas as the spice saffron. 
> 
> Human cultivation of saffron crocus and the trade and use of saffron 
> have endured for more than 3,500 years 
> and span different cultures, continents, and civilizations.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crocus%20sativus)
