# [[Pooideae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Pooideae,4662262]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Pooideae** are the largest subfamily of the grass family Poaceae, 
> with about 4,000 species in 15 tribes and roughly 200 genera. 
> 
> They include some major cereals such as wheat, barley, oat, rye 
> and many lawn and pasture grasses. 
> 
> They are often referred to as cool-season grasses, 
> because they are distributed in temperate climates. 
> 
> All of them use the C3 photosynthetic pathway.
>
> The Pooideae are the sister group of the bamboos within the BOP clade, 
> and are themselves subdivided into 15 tribes.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pooideae)
