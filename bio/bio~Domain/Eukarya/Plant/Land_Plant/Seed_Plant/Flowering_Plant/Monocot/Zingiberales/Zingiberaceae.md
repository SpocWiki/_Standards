# [[Zingiberaceae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Zingiberaceae,37021]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Zingiberaceae**, the ginger family, is a family of flowering plants containing 58 genera 
> and about 1600 described species distributed globally in the tropics and subtropics. 
> 
> They are aromatic perennial herbs with creeping rhizomes, sometimes tuberous. 
> 
> Many of the family's species are important ornamental, spice, or medicinal plants. 
> 
> Ornamental genera include the shell gingers (Alpinia), Curcuma, Hedychium, Kaempferia, and torch-gingers (Etlingera). Spices include ginger (Zingiber officinale), Thai ginger (Alpinia galanga and others), melegueta pepper (Aframomum melegueta), myoga (Zingiber mioga), korarima (Aframomum corrorima), turmeric (Curcuma), and cardamom (Amomum and Elettaria species).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zingiberaceae)
