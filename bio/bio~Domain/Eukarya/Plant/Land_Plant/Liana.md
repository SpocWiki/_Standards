# [[Liana]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Liana,14079]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Liana** is a long-stemmed  woody vine that is rooted in the soil at ground level 
> and uses trees, as well as other means of vertical support, 
> to climb up to the canopy in search of direct sunlight. 
> 
> The word liana does not refer to a taxonomic grouping, 
> but rather a habit of plant growth—much like tree or shrub. 
> 
> It comes from standard French liane, 
> itself from an Antilles French dialect word meaning to sheave.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liana)
