# Andwakiidae 

-   *[Andwakia](Andwakia)*
-   *[Synandwakia](Synandwakia)*

Containing group:[Athenaria](../Athenaria.md))

### Characteristics

Athenaria (Abasilaria) with elongate, cylindrical body, divisible into
physa, scapus and capitulum. Physa rounded but may be flattened. Scapus
sometimes with cinclides. Sphincter mesogloeal, long, usually very
strong. Mesenteries more or less distinctly divisible into macro- and
microcnemes. Not more than 6 pairs of perfect mesenteries, which are
fertile and have well developed filaments, strong retractors and
acontia. Acontia with basitrichs and microbasic amastigophors.
