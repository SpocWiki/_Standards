# [[Muridae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Muridae,25916]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Muridae**, or murids, are either the largest or second-largest family of rodents, 
> containing approximately 870 species, 
> including many species of mice, rats, and gerbils 
> found naturally throughout Eurasia, Africa, and Australia.
>
> The name Muridae comes from the Latin mus (genitive muris), meaning "mouse", 
> since all true mice belong to the family, 
> with the more typical mice belonging to the genus Mus.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muridae)
