# [[Pilosa]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Pilosa,143441]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Pilosa**  is a order of xenarthran placental mammals, native to the Americas. They includes anteaters and sloths (which include the extinct ground sloths). The name comes from the Latin word for "hairy".
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pilosa)
