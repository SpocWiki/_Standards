#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Testudinoidea,1946537]] 
 
## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Testudinoidea** is a superfamily within the suborder Cryptodira of the order Testudines. 
> 
> It includes 
> - the pond turtles (Family: Emydidae), 
> - Asian turtles (Family: Geoemydidae), 
> - the monotypic big-headed turtle (Family: Platysternidae), and 
> - the tortoises (Family: Testudinidae).
>
