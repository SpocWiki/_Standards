# [[Dasyuromorphia]]

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../_Standards/WikiData/WD~Dasyuromorphia,213333]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Dasyuromorphia** (meaning "hairy tail" in Greek) is an order 
> comprising most of the Australian carnivorous marsupials, including 
> quolls, dunnarts, the numbat, the Tasmanian devil, and the extinct thylacine. 
> 
> The order contains four families: one, the Myrmecobiidae, 
> with just a single living species (the numbat), 
> two with only extinct species (including the thylacine and Malleodectes), 
> and one, the Dasyuridae, with 73 extant species.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dasyuromorphia)
