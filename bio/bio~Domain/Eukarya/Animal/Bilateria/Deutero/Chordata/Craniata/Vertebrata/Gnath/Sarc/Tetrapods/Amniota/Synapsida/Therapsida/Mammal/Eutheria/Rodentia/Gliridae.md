# [[Gliridae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Gliridae,108235|WD~Gliridae,108235]] 
## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A dormouse is a rodent of the family **Gliridae** 
> (also variously called Myoxidae or Muscardinidae by different taxonomists). 
> 
> Dormice are nocturnal animals found in Africa, Asia, and Europe. They are named for their long, dormant hibernation period of six months or longer. There are 9 genera and 28 living species of dormice, with half of living species belonging to the African genus Graphiurus.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dormouse)
