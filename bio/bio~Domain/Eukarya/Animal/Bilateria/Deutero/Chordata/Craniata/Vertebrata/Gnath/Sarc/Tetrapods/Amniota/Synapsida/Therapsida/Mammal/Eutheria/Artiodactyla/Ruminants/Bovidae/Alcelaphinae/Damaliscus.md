# [[Damaliscus]] lunatus 

![ ](Damaliscus_lunatus0009163.jpg)

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Damaliscus lunatus** is a large African antelope of the genus Damaliscus a
> nd subfamily Alcelaphinae in the family Bovidae, 
> with a number of recognised geographic subspecies. 
> 
> Some authorities have split the different populations of the species into different species, 
> although this is seen as controversial. 
> 
> Common names include topi, sassaby (formerly also sassayby), tiang and tsessebe.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damaliscus%20lunatus)
