#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Lynx,677014]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Lynx** ( links; pl.: lynx or lynxes) is any of the four extant species 
> (the Canada lynx, Iberian lynx, Eurasian lynx and the bobcat) 
> within the medium-sized wild cat genus Lynx. 
> 
> The name originated in Middle English via Latin from the Greek word lynx (λύγξ), 
> derived from the Indo-European root leuk- ('light', 'brightness'), 
> in reference to the luminescence of its reflective eyes.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lynx)
