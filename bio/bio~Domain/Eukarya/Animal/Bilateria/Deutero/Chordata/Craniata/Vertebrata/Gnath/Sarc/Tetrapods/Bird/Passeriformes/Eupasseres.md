# [[Eupasseres]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Eupasseres,104864059]]

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Eupasserines are passerines in the clade **Eupasseres**. 
> The clade contains all passerines except the New Zealand wrens (Acanthisitti), 
> to which they are sister.
>
> The origin of the word is the prefix 'eu-', meaning 'true' or 'genuine', 
> and 'passeres',  referring to passerines. 
> So Eupasseres means 'true passerines', 
> as an exception to the ancient lineage of Acanthisitti.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eupasserine)
