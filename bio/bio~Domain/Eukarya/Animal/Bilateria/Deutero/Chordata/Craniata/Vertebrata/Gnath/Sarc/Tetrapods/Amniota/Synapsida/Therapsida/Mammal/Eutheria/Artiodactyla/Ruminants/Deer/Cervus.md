#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Cervus,644255]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Cervus** is a genus of deer that primarily are native to Eurasia, 
> although one species occurs in northern Africa and another in North America. 
> 
> In addition to the species presently placed in this genus, 
> it has included a whole range of other species now commonly placed in other genera. 
> Additionally, the species-level taxonomy is in a state of flux.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cervus)
