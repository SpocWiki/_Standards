# [[Rat]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~True_rats,36396]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Rats are various medium-sized, long-tailed rodents. 
> Species of rats are found throughout the order Rodentia, 
> but stereotypical rats are found in the genus Rattus. 
> 
> Other rat genera include Neotoma (pack rats), Bandicota (bandicoot rats) 
> and Dipodomys (kangaroo rats).
>
> Rats are typically distinguished from mice by their size. 
> Usually the common name of a large muroid rodent will include the word "rat", 
> while a smaller muroid's name will include "mouse". 
> 
> The common terms rat and mouse are not taxonomically specific. 
> There are 56 known species of rats in the world.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rat)
