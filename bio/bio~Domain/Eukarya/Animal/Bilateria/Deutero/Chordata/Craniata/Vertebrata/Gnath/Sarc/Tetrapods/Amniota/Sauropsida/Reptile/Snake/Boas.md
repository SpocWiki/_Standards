## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Boidae**, commonly known as boas or boids, are a family of nonvenomous snakes 
> primarily found in the Americas, as well as Africa, Europe, Asia, and some Pacific islands. 
> 
> Boas include some of the world's largest snakes, 
> with the green anaconda of South America being the heaviest and second-longest snake known; 
> in general, adults are medium to large in size, with females usually larger than the males. 
> Six subfamilies comprising 14-15 genera and 54-67 species are currently recognized.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boidae)
