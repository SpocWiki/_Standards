# [[Macropodidae]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[Macropodidae,]]

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Macropodidae** is a family of marsupials that includes kangaroos, wallabies, 
> tree-kangaroos, wallaroos, pademelons, quokkas, and several other groups. 
> 
> These genera are allied to the suborder Macropodiformes, containing other macropods, 
> and are native to the Australian continent (the mainland and Tasmania), New Guinea and nearby islands.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macropodidae)
