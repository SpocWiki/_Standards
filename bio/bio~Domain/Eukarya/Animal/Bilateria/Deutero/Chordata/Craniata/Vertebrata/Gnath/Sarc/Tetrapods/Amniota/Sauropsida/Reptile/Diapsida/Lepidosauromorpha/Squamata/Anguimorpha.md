# [[Anguimorpha]] 



## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Anguimorpha** is a suborder of squamates. 
> The group was named by Fürbringer in 1900 
> to include all autarchoglossans closer to Varanus and Anguis than Scincus. 
> These lizards, along with iguanians and snakes, 
> constitute the proposed "venom clade" Toxicofera of all venomous reptiles.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anguimorpha)
