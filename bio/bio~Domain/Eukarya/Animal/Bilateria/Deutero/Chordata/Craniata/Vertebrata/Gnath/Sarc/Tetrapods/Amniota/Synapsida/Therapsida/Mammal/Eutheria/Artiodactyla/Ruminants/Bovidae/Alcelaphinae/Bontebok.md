# [[Bontebok]] 

![ ](243614700_b757a29021_b.jpg)

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The bontebok (**Damaliscus pygargus**) is an antelope found in South Africa, Lesotho and Namibia. 
> 
> D. pygargus has two subspecies; the nominate subspecies (D. p. pygargus), occurring naturally in the Fynbos and Renosterveld areas of the Western Cape, and the blesbok (D. p. phillipsi) occurring in the Highveld.
>
> The bontebok is related to the common tsessebe.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bontebok)
