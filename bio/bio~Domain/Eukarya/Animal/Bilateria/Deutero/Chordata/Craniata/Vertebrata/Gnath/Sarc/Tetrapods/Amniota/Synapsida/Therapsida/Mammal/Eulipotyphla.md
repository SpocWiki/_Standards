# [[Eulipotyphla]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Eulipotyphla,16635184]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Eulipotyphla** (, from eu- + Lipotyphla, meaning truly lacking blind gut; 
> sometimes called true insectivores) is an order of mammals comprising the Erinaceidae 
> (hedgehogs and gymnures); Solenodontidae (solenodons); 
> Talpidae (moles, shrew-like moles and desmans); and Soricidae (true shrews) families.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eulipotyphla)
