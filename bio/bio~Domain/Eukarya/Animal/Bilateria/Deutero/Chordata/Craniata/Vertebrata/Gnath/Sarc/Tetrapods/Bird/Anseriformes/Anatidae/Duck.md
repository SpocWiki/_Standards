## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Duck** is the common name for numerous species of waterfowl in the family Anatidae. 
> Ducks are generally smaller and shorter-necked than swans and geese, 
> which are members of the same family. 
> 
> Divided among several subfamilies, they are a form taxon; 
> they do not represent a monophyletic group 
> (the group of all descendants of a single common ancestral species), 
> since swans and geese are not considered ducks. 
> Ducks are mostly aquatic birds, and may be found in both fresh water and sea water.
>
> Ducks are sometimes confused with several types of unrelated water birds with similar forms, such as loons or divers, grebes, gallinules and coots.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Duck)
