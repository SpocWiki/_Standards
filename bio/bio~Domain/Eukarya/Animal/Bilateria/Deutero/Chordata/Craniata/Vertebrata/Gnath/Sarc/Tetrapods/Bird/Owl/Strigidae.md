## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The true owls or typical owls (family **Strigidae**) are one of the two generally accepted families of owls, 
> the other being the barn owls and bay owls (Tytonidae).  
> 
> This large family comprises 230 living or recently extinct species in 24 genera. 
> The Strigidae owls have a cosmopolitan distribution and are found on every continent except Antarctica.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strigidae)
