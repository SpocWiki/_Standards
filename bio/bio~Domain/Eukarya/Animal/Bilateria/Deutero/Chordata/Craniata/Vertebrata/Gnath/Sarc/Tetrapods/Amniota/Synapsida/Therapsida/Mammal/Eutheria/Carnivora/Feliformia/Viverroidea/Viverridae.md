## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Viverridae** is a family of small to medium-sized feliform mammals, 
> comprising 14 genera with 33 species. 
> 
> This family was named and first described by John Edward Gray in 1821. 
> Viverrids occur all over Africa, in southern Europe, 
> and in South and Southeast Asia on both sides of the Wallace Line.
>
> The species of the subfamily Genettinae are known as genets and oyans. 
> The viverrids of the subfamily Viverrinae are commonly called civets; 
> the Paradoxurinae and most Hemigalinae species are called palm civets.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Viverridae)
