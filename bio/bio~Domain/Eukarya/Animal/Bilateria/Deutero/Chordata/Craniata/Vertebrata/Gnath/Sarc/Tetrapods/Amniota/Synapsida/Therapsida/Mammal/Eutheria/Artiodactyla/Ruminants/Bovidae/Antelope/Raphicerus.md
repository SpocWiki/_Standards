# [[Raphicerus]] 

![ ](1703steenbok.jpg)
![Steinbuck](steinbuck.jpg)

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Raphicerus** is a genus of small antelopes of the tribe Neotragini (subfamily Antilopinae).
>
> Raphicerus is endemic to sub-Saharan Africa, 
> ranging from Kenya in the north to the Western Cape in South Africa.
>
> The genus contains three species:
> - Cape or southern grysbok R. melanotis
> - Sharpe's or northern grysbok R. sharpei
> - Steenbok R. campestris
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Raphicerus)
