## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The tribe **Dasyurini** includes several genera of small carnivorous marsupials 
> native to Australia: quolls, kowari, mulgara, kaluta, dibblers, neophascogales, pseudantechinuses, and the Tasmanian devil.
