# [[Platycopioida]]

Platycopiidae 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Platycopiidae is a family of copepods. 
> Until the description of Nanocopia in 1988, it contained the single genus Platycopia. 
> 
> It now contains four genera, three of which are monotypic; 
> the exception is Platycopia, with 8 species.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Platycopiidae)
