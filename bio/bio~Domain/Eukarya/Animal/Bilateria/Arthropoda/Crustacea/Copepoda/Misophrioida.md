## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Misophrioida** is an order of copepods, containing the following families:
>
> Misophriidae Boxshall & Jaume, 2000
> Palpophriidae Boxshall & Jaume, 2000
> Speleophriidae Boxshall & Jaume, 2000
>
