# Aegla leptochela 

![Aegla leptochela](leptochela/leptochela.jpg)  

Containing group:[Aeglidae](../Aeglidae.md))

### Types

[Keith A. Crandall]()

Holotype, *Museum National D\'Histoire Naturelle, Paris, Franca* 3488 (1 male).

Paratypes, *Museum National D\'Histoire Naturelle, Paris, Franca* 1986 (11 males, 15 females).

### Type Locality

Near the entrance to Gruta dos Paiva, Fazenda Intervales, Iporanga, Sao
Paulo, Brazil.

### Distribution

Gruta dos Paiva, Iporanga, Sao Paulo, Brazil.
