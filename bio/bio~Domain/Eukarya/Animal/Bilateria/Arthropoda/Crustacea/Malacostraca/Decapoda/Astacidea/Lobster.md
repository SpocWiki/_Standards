# [[Lobster]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Nephropidae,1038113]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Lobsters are malacostracan decapod crustaceans 
> of the family **Nephropidae** or its synonym Homaridae. 
> 
> They have long bodies with muscular tails and live in crevices or burrows on the sea floor. 
> Three of their five pairs of legs have claws, including the first pair, 
> which are usually much larger than the others. 
> 
> Highly prized as seafood, lobsters are economically important 
> and are often one of the most profitable commodities in the coastal areas they populate.
>
> Commercially important species include two species of Homarus 
> - the Northern Hemisphere genus Nephrops from the northern Atlantic Ocean 
> - and scampi, the Southern Hemisphere genus Metanephrops 
>   (which look more like a shrimp, or a "mini lobster").
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lobster)
