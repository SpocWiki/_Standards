# [[Cambaroides]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Cambaroides** is a genus of freshwater crayfish from eastern Asia 
> (eastern Russia, northeastern China, Korean Peninsula and Japan). 
> Together with Pontastacus, they are the only crayfish native to Asia.
