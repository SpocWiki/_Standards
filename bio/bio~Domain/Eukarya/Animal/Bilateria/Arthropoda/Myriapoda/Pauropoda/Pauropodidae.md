# [[Pauropodidae]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Pauropodidae** is the most diverse family of pauropods, 
> containing 27 genera and more than 800 species. 
> 
> This family has a subcosmopolitan distribution. 
> These pauropods usually live in the soil on mountains and hills. 
> This family also includes the only known fossil pauropod (Eopauropus).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pauropodidae)
