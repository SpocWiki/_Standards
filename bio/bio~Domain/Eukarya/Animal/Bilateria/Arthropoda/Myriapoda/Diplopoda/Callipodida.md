# [[Callipodida]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Callipodida** is an order of millipedes containing around 130 species, 
> many characterized by crests or ridges.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Callipodida)
