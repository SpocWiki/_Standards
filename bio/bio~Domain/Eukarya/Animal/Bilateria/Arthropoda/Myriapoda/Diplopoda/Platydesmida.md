# [[Platydesmida]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Platydesmida** (Greek for platy "flat" and desmos "bond") is an order of millipedes 
> containing two families and over 60 species. 
> Some species practice paternal care, in which males guard the eggs.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Platydesmida)
