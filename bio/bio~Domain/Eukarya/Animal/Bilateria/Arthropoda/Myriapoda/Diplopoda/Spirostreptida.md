# [[Spirostreptida]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Spirostreptida** is an order of long, cylindrical millipedes. 
> There are approximately 1000 described species, 
> making Spirostreptida the third largest order of millipedes 
> after Polydesmida and Chordeumatida.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spirostreptida)
