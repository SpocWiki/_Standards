# [[Dictynoidea]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Dictynoidea**  or dictynoids are a group of araneomorph spiders 
> that have been treated as a superfamily. 
> 
> The composition of the group has varied. 
> Phylogenetic studies in the 21st century have 
> failed to confirm the monophyly of the dictynoids as originally defined.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dictynoidea)
