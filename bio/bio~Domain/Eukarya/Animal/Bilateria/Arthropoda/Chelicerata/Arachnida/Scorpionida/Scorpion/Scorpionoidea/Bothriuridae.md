# [[Bothriuridae]] 

![](Bothriuridae.png)

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Bothriuridae** are a family of scorpions, comprising 151 species in 16 genera.
>
> The family has representatives in temperate and subtropical habitats from four continents: 
> South America, Africa, Asia, and Australia. 
> One genus (Cercophonius) has recently been discovered in the Himalayas.
>
> The members of this family have a unique feature - 
> the normally pentagonal sternum consists of two transverse bars 
> (except Liposoma and Tehuankea) and is several times broader than long.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bothriuridae)
