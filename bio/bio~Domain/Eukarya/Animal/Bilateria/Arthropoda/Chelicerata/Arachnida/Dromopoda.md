# [[Dromopoda]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Dromopoda,5308296]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Dromopoda** is a proposed subclass of the arachnids, including 
> Opiliones (harvestmen), Scorpions, Pseudoscorpions and Solifugae ("camel spiders"). 
> 
> The latter three are sometimes grouped as Novogenuata. 
> This taxon was first proposed by Jeffrey Shultz in 1990 based on morphological analysis. 
> 
> A combined morphological and molecular analysis in 1998 supported the monophyly of Dromopoda. 
> 
> However, subsequent molecular and combined analyses have not supported the monophyly of Dromopoda.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dromopoda)
