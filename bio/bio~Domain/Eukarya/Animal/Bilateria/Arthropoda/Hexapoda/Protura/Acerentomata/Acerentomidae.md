# [[Acerentomidae]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Acerentomidae** are a family of hexapods in the order Protura. 
> Acerentomids are not tracheated, and instead use cuticular gas exchange.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acerentomidae)
