# [[Antelientomon]]

## Antelientomidae 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Antelientomon** is a genus of hexapods in the order Protura, 
> and the only genus in the family Antelientomidae. 
> It contains three species.
> - Antelientomon guilinicum Zhang & Yin, 1981
> - Antelientomon prodromi Yin, 1974
> - Antelientomon xizangnicum Yin, 1990
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antelientomon)
