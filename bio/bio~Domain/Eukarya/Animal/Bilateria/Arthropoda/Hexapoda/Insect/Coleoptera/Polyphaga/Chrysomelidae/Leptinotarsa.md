# [[Leptinotarsa]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[_Standards/WikiData/WD~Leptinotarsa,602990]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Leptinotarsa** is a genus of leaf beetles.
>
> Several species in the genus produce leptinotarsin, 
> a toxin similar to the diamphotoxin produced by species of the African leaf beetle genus Diamphidia. 
> Some Leptinotarsa species are parasitised by ground beetles of genus Lebia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leptinotarsa)
