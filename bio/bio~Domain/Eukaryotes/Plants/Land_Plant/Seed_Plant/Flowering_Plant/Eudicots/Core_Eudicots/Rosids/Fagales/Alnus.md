## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Alders are trees of the genus **Alnus** in the birch family Betulaceae. 
> The genus includes about 35 species of monoecious trees and shrubs, 
> a few reaching a large size, distributed throughout the north temperate zone 
> with a few species extending into Central America, as well as the northern and southern Andes.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alder)
