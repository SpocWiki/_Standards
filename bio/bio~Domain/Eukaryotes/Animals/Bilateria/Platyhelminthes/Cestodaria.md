# [[Cestodaria]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Cestodaria** is one of two subclasses of the class Cestoda.  The Cestodaria subclass is made up of Amphilinidea and Gyrocotylidea. The larvae have ten hooks on the posterior end.
>
