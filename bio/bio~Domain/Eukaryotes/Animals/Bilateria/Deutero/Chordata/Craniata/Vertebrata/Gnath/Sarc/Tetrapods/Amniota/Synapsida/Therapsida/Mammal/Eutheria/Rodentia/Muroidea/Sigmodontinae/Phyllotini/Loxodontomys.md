## Introduction

Originally created as a subgenus of **Phyllotis** by Osgood (1947), then
transferred to **Auliscomys** by Simonetti and Spotorno (1980), but
resurrected asa genus by Braun (1993) based on cladistic and phenetic
analysis of morphological characters. Generic status was strongly
supported by a cladistic study in Steppan (1993; 1995).
