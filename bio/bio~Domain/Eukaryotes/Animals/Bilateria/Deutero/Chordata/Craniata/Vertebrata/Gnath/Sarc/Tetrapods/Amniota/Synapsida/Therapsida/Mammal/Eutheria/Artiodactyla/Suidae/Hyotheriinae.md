# [[Hyotheriinae]]

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Hyotheriinae** was a subfamily of even-toed ungulates 
> that existed during the Miocene and Pliocene in Europe, Asia, and Africa.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyotheriinae)
