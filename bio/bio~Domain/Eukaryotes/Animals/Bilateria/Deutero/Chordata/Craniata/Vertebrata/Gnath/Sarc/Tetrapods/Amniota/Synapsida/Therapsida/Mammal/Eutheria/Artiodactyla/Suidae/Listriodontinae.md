# [[Listriodontinae]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Listriodontinae** was an extinct subfamily of even-toed ungulates 
> that existed during the Miocene in Europe, Africa, and Asia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Listriodontinae)
