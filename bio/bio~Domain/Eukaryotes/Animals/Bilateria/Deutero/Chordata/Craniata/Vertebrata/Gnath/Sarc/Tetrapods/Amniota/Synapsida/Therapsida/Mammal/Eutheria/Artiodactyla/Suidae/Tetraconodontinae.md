# [[Tetraconodontinae]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Tetraconodontinae** is an extinct subfamily of the pig family (Suidae). 
> Fossils have been found in Africa and Asia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tetraconodontinae)
