# [[Glomeridesmus]]

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Glomeridesmida** is an order of millipedes in the infraclass Pentazonia 
> containing two families (Glomeridesmidae and Termitodesmidae) 
> and at least 35 described species. 
> 
> Glomeridesmida is the only living order of the superorder Limacomorpha. 
> Glomeridesmidans are also known as slug millipedes.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glomeridesmida)
