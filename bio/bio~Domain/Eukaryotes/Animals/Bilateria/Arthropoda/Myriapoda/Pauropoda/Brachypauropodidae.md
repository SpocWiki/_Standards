# [[Brachypauropodidae]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Brachypauropodidae** is a family of pauropods. This family has a nearly worldwide distribution. 
> Pauropods in this family are found on all continents except South America and Antarctica.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brachypauropodidae)
