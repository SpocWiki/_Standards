# [[Eurypauropodidae]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Eurypauropodidae** is a family of pauropods. 
> The family was first described by the American zoologist John A. Ryder during October 1873. 
> Eurypauropodids are generally less than 1 millimeter long, 
> and their outer plates are usually completely sclerotised.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eurypauropodidae)
