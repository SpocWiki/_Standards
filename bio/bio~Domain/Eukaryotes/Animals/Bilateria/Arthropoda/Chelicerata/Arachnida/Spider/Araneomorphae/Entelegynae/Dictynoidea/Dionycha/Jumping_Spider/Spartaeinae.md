# [[Spartaeinae]] 

## Introduction

The spartaeines are unusual salticids that lie phylogenetically outside
the largest group of salticids, and thus are considered to lie near the
base of the family. Along with the lyssomanines they lack many of the
derived features that mark the bulk of the family (the Salticine
Division). The group was reviewed by Wanless (1984).
