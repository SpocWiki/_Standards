# [[Eosentomidae]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Eosentomidae** are a family of hexapods in the order Protura. 
> They are tracheated, unlike the Acerentomidae.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eosentomidae)
