# *Pinus remota*

## paper-shell pinyon 

Containing group: [subsection Cembroides](Eukaryotes/Green_plants/Embryophytes/Spermatopsida/Conifers/Pinaceae/Pinus/Strobus/Cembroides.md)

### Introduction

This pinyon\'s thin, easily ruptured seed coat, tasty endosperm, and
hardy character have long recommended it for economic development. It
forms scattered stands in Texas, Coahuila, Durango, and Nuevo Leon.
