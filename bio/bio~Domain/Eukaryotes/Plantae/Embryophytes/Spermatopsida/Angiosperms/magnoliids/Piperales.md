# Piperales 

![](Aristolochia_clematitis_002.jpg)
![](Piper_nigrum_002.jpg)

Relationships after Qiu et al. (1999, 2000), Savolainen et al. (2000)
and Soltis et al. (2000)

Containing group: [magnoliids](magnoliids.md)

### Information on the Internet

[Parasitic Plant Connection: Hydnoraceae](http://www.science.siu.edu/parasitic-plants/Hydnoraceae/)
