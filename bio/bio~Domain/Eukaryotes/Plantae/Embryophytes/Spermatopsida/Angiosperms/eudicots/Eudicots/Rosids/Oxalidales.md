# Oxalidales 

![](Cephalotus_follicularis.jpg)
![](Crinodendron_hookeranum_002.jpg)

Relationships after Matthews & Endress (2002), Savolainen & al. (2000b),
Soltis & al. (2000).

Containing group: [Rosids](Rosids.md)
