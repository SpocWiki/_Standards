# Solanales 

![Calystegia dahurica](Calystegia_dahurica.jpg)
![](Physalis_crassifolia_002.jpg)

Containing group: [Asterids](Asterids.md)

### Information on the Internet

[Botanical Garden of Nijmegen](http://www-bgard.sci.kun.nl/). Most
extensive ex situ plant collection of non-tuberous Solanaceae species in
the world.
