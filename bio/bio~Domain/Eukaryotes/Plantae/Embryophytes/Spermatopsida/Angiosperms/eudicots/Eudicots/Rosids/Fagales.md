# Fagales 

![Red Alder, Alnus rubra](Alnus_rubra0427.jpg)
![](Quercus_douglasii0086.jpg)
![](Juglans_regia2580_002.jpg)

Relationships after Manos et al. (1993), Manos and Steele (1997), and
Hilu et al. (2003).

Containing group: [Rosids](Rosids.md)
