## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In botany, a **Fruit** is the seed-bearing structure in flowering plants (angiosperms) 
> that is formed from the ovary after flowering (see Fruit anatomy).
>
> In botanical usage, the term fruit also includes many structures 
> that are not commonly called as such **in everyday language**, 
> such as nuts, bean pods, corn kernels, tomatoes, and wheat grains.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fruit)
