## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Plant anatomy or phytotomy is the general term 
> for the study of the internal structure of plants. 
> 
> Originally, it included plant morphology, 
> the description of the physical form and external structure of plants, 
> but since the mid-20th century, plant anatomy has been considered a separate field 
> referring **only to internal plant structure**. 
> 
> Plant anatomy is now frequently investigated at the cellular level, 
> and often involves the sectioning of tissues and microscopy.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plant%20anatomy)
