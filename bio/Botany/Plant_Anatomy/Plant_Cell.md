# [[Plant_Cell]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Plant_cell,1056194]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Plant cell**s are the cells present in green plants, 
> photosynthetic eukaryotes of the kingdom Plantae. 
> 
> Their distinctive features include 
> - primary cell walls containing cellulose, hemicelluloses and pectin, 
> - the presence of plastids with the capability to perform photosynthesis and store starch, 
> - a large vacuole that regulates turgor pressure, 
> - the absence of flagella or centrioles, except in the gametes, and 
> - a unique method of cell division involving the formation of a cell plate or phragmoplast 
>   that separates the new daughter cells.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plant%20cell)
