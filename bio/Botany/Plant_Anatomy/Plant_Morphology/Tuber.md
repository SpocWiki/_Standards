#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../WikiData/WD~Tuber,183319|WD~Tuber,183319]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Tuber**s are a type of enlarged structure 
> that plants use as storage organs for nutrients, derived from stems or roots. 
> 
> Tubers help plants perennate (survive winter or dry months), 
> provide energy and nutrients, and are a means of asexual reproduction.
>
> Stem tubers manifest as thickened rhizomes (underground stems) 
> or stolons (horizontal connections between organisms); 
> examples include the potato and yam. 
> 
> The term root tuber describes modified lateral roots, 
> as in sweet potato, cassava, and dahlia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tuber)
