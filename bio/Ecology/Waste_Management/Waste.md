## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Waste** are unwanted or unusable materials. 
> 
> Waste is any substance discarded after primary use, 
> or is worthless, defective and of no use. 
> 
> A by-product, by contrast is a joint product of relatively minor economic value. 
> A waste product may become a by-product, joint product or resource 
> through an invention that raises a waste product's value above zero.
>
> Examples include 
> - municipal solid waste (household trash/refuse), 
> - hazardous waste, 
> - wastewater (such as sewage, which contains bodily wastes (feces and urine) and surface runoff), 
> - radioactive waste, and others.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Waste)
