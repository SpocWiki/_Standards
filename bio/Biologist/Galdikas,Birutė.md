# [[Galdikas,Birutė]] 



## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Biruté Marija Filomena Galdikas or Biruté Mary Galdikas, OC (born 10 May 1946), 
> is a Lithuanian-Canadian anthropologist, primatologist, conservationist, ethologist, and author. 
> 
> She is a professor at Simon Fraser University. 
> 
> In the field of primatology, Galdikas is recognized as a leading authority on orangutans. 
> 
> Prior to her field study of orangutans, scientists knew little about the species.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Birut%C3%A9%20Galdikas)
