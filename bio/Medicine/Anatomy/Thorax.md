# [[Thorax]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../WikiData/WD~Thorax,9645|WD~Thorax,9645]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Thorax** (pl.: thoraces or thoraxes) or chest 
> is a part of the anatomy of mammals and other tetrapod animals 
> located between the neck and the abdomen.
>
> In insects, crustaceans, and the extinct trilobites, 
> the thorax is one of the three main divisions of the body, 
> each in turn composed of multiple segments.
>
> The human thorax includes the thoracic cavity and the thoracic wall. 
> It contains organs including the heart, lungs, and thymus gland, 
> as well as muscles and various other internal structures. 
> 
> The chest may be affected by many diseases, of which the most common symptom is chest pain.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thorax)
