## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Urobilin**, also known as urochrome, 
> is the chemical primarily responsible for the yellow color of urine. 
> It is a linear tetrapyrrole compound that, 
> along with the related colorless compound urobilinogen, 
> are degradation products of the cyclic tetrapyrrole heme.
>
