# [[Nail]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Nail,36864]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Nail** is a protective plate characteristically found at the tip of the digits 
> (fingers and toes) of almost all primates (exception: Marmosets), 
> corresponding to the claws in other tetrapod animals. 
> 
> Fingernails and toenails are made of a tough rigid protein called alpha-keratin, 
> a polymer also found in the claws, hooves, and horns of vertebrates.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nail%20(anatomy))
