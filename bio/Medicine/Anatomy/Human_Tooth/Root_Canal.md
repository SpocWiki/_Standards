# [[Root_Canal]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Root_canal,848390]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A root canal is the naturally occurring  anatomic space within the root of a tooth. 
> It consists of the pulp chamber (within the coronal part of the tooth), 
> the main canal(s), and more intricate anatomical branches 
> that may connect the root canals to each other or to the surface of the root.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Root%20canal)
