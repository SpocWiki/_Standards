## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Soap** is a salt of a fatty acid (sometimes other carboxylic acids) 
> used for cleaning and lubricating products as well as other applications. 
> 
> In a domestic setting, soaps, specifically "toilet soaps", are surfactants 
> usually used for washing, bathing, and other types of housekeeping. 
> 
> In industrial settings, soaps are used as thickeners, 
> components of some lubricants, emulsifiers, and catalysts.
>
> Soaps are often produced by mixing fats and oils with a base. 
> 
> Humans have used soap for millennia; 
> evidence exists for the production of soap-like materials 
> in ancient Babylon around 2800 BC.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soap)
