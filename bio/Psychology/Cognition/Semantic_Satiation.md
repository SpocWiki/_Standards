# [[Semantic_Satiation]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Semantic satiation** is a psychological phenomenon in which 
> repetition causes a word or phrase to temporarily lose meaning for the listener, 
> who then perceives the speech as repeated meaningless sounds. 
> 
> Extended inspection or analysis (staring at the word or phrase for a long time) 
> in place of repetition also produces the same effect.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semantic%20satiation)  

This can be used in CBT to reduce Trigger-Effects.
