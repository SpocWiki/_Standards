# [[Pessimism]] 

#is_/opposite_of :: [[Optimism]]  
#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Pessimism,484954]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Pessimism** is a mental attitude in which 
> an undesirable outcome is **anticipated** from a given situation. 
> 
> Pessimists tend to focus on the negatives of life in general. 
> A common question asked to test for pessimism is "Is the glass half empty or half full?"; 
> - in this situation, a pessimist is said to see the glass as half empty, 
>   or in extreme cases completely empty, 
> - while an optimist is said to see the glass as half full. 
> 
> Throughout history, the pessimistic disposition has had effects on all major areas of thinking.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pessimism)
