#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../WikiData/WD~Shyness,913668|WD~Shyness,913668]]    

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Shyness** (also called diffidence) is the feeling of apprehension, lack of comfort, 
> or awkwardness especially when a person is around other people. 
> 
> This commonly occurs in new situations or with unfamiliar people; 
> a shy person may simply opt to avoid these situations. 
> 
> Although shyness can be a characteristic of people who have low self-esteem, 
> the primary defining characteristic of shyness is 
> a fear of what other people will think of a person's behavior. 
> 
> This fear of negative reactions such as being mocked, humiliated or patronized, 
> criticized or rejected can cause a shy person to retreat. 
> 
> Stronger forms of shyness can be referred to as social anxiety or social phobia.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shyness)
