## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Charisma** is a personal quality of magnetic charm, persuasion, or appeal. 
>
> In the fields of sociology and political science, psychology, and management, 
> the term charismatic describes a type of leadership. 
>
> In Christian theology, the term charisma appears as the Spiritual gift (charism) 
> which is an endowment with an extraordinary power given by the Holy Spirit.
>
