This dimension captures sincerity, fairness, greed avoidance, and modesty.
Honesty–Humility predicts outcomes like unethical decision-making, corruption risk, and exploitative behavior,
not covered by the Big Five. 

Agreeableness captures compassion and politeness but lacks exploitative-behavior variance.  
Approximately 93.75 % of Honesty–Humility variance remains unexplained by the Big Five.
