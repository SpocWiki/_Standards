## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Equanimity is a state of psychological stability and composure 
> which is undisturbed by the experience of or exposure to emotions, pain, 
> or other phenomena that may otherwise cause a loss of mental balance. 
> 
> The virtue and value of equanimity 
> is extolled and advocated by a number of major [[../../../../Philosophy/Metaphysic/Religion|Religion]]s and ancient [[../../../../Philosophy|Philosophies]].
>
