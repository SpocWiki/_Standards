## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Psychiatrist** is a physician who specializes in psychiatry. 
> Psychiatrists are physicians who evaluate patients 
> to determine whether their symptoms are the result of a physical illness, 
> a combination of physical and mental ailments or strictly mental issues. 
> 
> Sometimes a psychiatrist works within a multi-disciplinary team, 
> which may comprise clinical psychologists, social workers, occupational therapists, 
> and nursing staff. 
> 
> Psychiatrists have broad training in a biopsychosocial approach to the 
> assessment and management of mental illness.
>
> As part of the clinical assessment process, psychiatrists may employ a mental status examination; 
> a physical examination; brain imaging such as a computerized tomography, 
> magnetic resonance imaging, or positron emission tomography scan; and blood testing. 
> 
> Psychiatrists use pharmacologic, psychotherapeutic, or interventional approaches 
> to treat mental disorders.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Psychiatrist)
