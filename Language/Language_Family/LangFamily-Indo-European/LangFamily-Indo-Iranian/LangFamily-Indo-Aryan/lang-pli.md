## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Pāli is a Middle Indo-Aryan language 
> that is widely studied as the sacred language of Theravada Buddhism 
> and the language of the Tipiṭaka . 
> 
> **Pali** was designated a classical language of India 
> by the Government of India on 3 October 2024.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pali)
