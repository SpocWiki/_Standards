#is_/same_as :: [[../../../WikiData/WD~Trans–New_Guinea,34018|WD~Trans–New_Guinea,34018]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Trans–New Guinea (TNG) is an extensive family of Papuan languages 
> spoken on the island of New Guinea and neighboring islands, 
> a region corresponding to the country Papua New Guinea as well as parts of Indonesia.
>
> Trans–New Guinea is perhaps 
> the third-largest language family in the world by number of languages. 
> 
> The core of the family is considered to be established, 
> but its boundaries and overall membership are uncertain. 
> 
> The languages are spoken by around 3 million people. 
> There have been several main proposals as to its internal classification.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trans%E2%80%93New%20Guinea%20languages)
