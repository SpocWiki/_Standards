# [[Somali_Language]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../WikiData/WD~Somali,13275|WD~Somali,13275]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Somali (sə-MAH-lee, soh-; Af Soomaali; Wadaad: اف صومالِ‎; Osmanya: 𐒖𐒍 𐒈𐒝𐒑𐒛𐒐𐒘 [af soːmaːli]) 
> is an Afroasiatic language belonging to the [[LangFamily~Cushitic]] branch. 
> 
> It is spoken primarily in Greater Somalia, and by the Somali diaspora as a mother tongue. 
> Somali is an official language in both Somalia and Ethiopia, 
> and serves as a national language in Djibouti. 
> It is also a recognised minority language in Kenya. 
> 
> The **Somali language** is officially written with the Latin alphabet, 
> although the Arabic script and several Somali scripts are informally used like 
> Osmanya, Kaddare and the Borama script .
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Somali%20language)
