## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In a writing system, a **Letter** is a grapheme that generally corresponds to a [[../Speech/Phoneme|phoneme]]—
> the smallest functional unit of speech—
> though there is rarely total one-to-one correspondence between the two. 
> An alphabet is a writing system that uses letters.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Letter%20(alphabet))
