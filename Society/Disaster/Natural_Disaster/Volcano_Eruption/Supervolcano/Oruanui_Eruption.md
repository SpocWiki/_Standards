# [[Oruanui_Eruption]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Oruanui_eruption,2745099]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Oruanui eruption** (also known as the Kawakawa eruption or Kawakawa/Oruanui event) 
> of Taupō Volcano in New Zealand around 25,700 years before present 
> was the world's most recent supereruption, 
> and its largest phreatomagmatic eruption characterised to date.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oruanui%20eruption)
