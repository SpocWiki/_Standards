## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Samuel Moore Walton (March 29, 1918 – April 5, 1992) was an American business magnate 
> best known for co-founding the retailers Walmart and Sam's Club, 
> which he started in Rogers, Arkansas, and Midwest City, Oklahoma, 
> in 1962 and 1983 respectively. 
> 
> Wal-Mart Stores Inc. grew to be the world's largest corporation by revenue 
> as well as the biggest private employer in the world. 
> 
> For a period of time, Walton was the richest person in the United States. 
> His family has remained the richest family in the U.S. for several consecutive years, 
> with a net worth of around $440.6 billion US as of January 2025. 
> 
> In 1992 at the age of 74, Walton died of blood cancer 
> and was buried at the Bentonville Cemetery in his longtime home of Bentonville, Arkansas.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam%20Walton)
