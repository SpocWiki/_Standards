## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Consortium** (pl. consortiums or consortia) is an association of two or more individuals, 
> companies, organizations, or governments (or any combination of these entities) 
> with the objective of participating in a common activity 
> or pooling their resources for achieving a common goal. 
> 
> Consortia are generally nonprofit 
> with a goal to help its members improve their competitiveness in the specific field.
>
> Consortium is a Latin word meaning "partnership", "association", or "society", 
> and derives from consors ("shared in property"), itself from con- ("together") and sors ("fate").
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Consortium)
