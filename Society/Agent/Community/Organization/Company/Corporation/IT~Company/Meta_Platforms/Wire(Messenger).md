## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Wire is an encrypted communication and collaboration app created by Wire Swiss. 
> It is available for iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, Linux, and web browsers such as Firefox. 
> 
> Wire offers a collaboration suite featuring messenger, voice calls, video calls, conference calls, 
> file-sharing, and external collaboration – all protected by a secure end-to-end-encryption. 
> 
> Wire offers three options, built on its security technology: 
> - Wire Pro – which offers Wire's collaboration feature for businesses, 
> - Wire Enterprise – which includes Wire Pro capabilities 
>   with added features for large-scale or regulated organizations, and 
> - Wire Red – an on-demand crisis collaboration suite. 
> - They also offer Wire Personal, a secure messaging app for personal use.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wire%20(software))
