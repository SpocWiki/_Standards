## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **nightclub** or dance club  is a club that is open at night, 
> usually for drinking, dancing and other entertainment. 
> 
> Nightclubs often have a bar and discotheque (usually simply known as disco) 
> with a dance floor, laser lighting displays, and a stage for live music or a disc jockey (DJ) 
> who mixes recorded music. 
> 
> Nightclubs tend to be smaller than live music venues like theatres and stadiums, 
> with few or no seats for customers.
>
> Nightclubs generally restrict access to people in terms of 
> age, attire, personal belongings, and behaviors. 
> 
> Nightclubs typically have dress codes 
> to prohibit people wearing informal, indecent, offensive, gym, or gang-related attire from entering. 
> 
> Unlike other entertainment venues, 
> nightclubs are more likely to use bouncers to screen prospective patrons for entry.
>
> The busiest nights for a nightclub are Friday and Saturday nights. 
> Most nightclubs cater to a particular music genre or sound for branding effects. 
> Some nightclubs may offer food and beverages (including alcoholic beverages).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nightclub)
