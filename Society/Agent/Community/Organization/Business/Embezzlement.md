## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Embezzlement** (from Anglo-Norman, from Old French besillier ("to torment, etc."), of unknown origin) 
> is a type of financial crime, usually involving theft of money from a business or employer. 
> It often involves a trusted individual taking advantage of their position 
> to steal funds or assets, most commonly over a period of time.
>
