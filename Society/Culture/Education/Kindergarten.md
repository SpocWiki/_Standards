# [[Kindergarten]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Kindergarten,126807]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Kindergarten** is a preschool educational approach based on playing, singing, practical activities 
> such as drawing, and social interaction as part of the transition from home to school. 
> 
> Such institutions were originally made in the late 18th century in Germany and Alsace 
> to serve children whose parents both worked outside home. 
> 
> The term was coined by German pedagogue Friedrich Fröbel, 
> whose approach globally influenced early-years education. 
> 
> Today, the term is used in many countries to describe a variety of educational institutions 
> and learning spaces for children ranging from two to six years of age, based on a variety of teaching methods.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kindergarten)
