## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Student** is a person enrolled in a school or other educational institution, 
> or more generally, a person who takes a special interest in a subject.
>
> In the United Kingdom and most commonwealth countries, 
> a "student" attends a secondary school or higher (e.g., college or university); 
> those in primary or elementary schools are "pupils".
>
