#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../WikiData/WD~Volunteering,188844|WD~Volunteering,188844]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Volunteering** is an optional and freely chosen act of an individual or group 
> giving their time and labor, often for community service. 
> 
> Many volunteers have specialized training in the fields that they work in, 
> such as medicine, education, or emergency rescue. 
> 
> Others provide their services as needed, such as in response to a natural disaster.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Volunteering)
