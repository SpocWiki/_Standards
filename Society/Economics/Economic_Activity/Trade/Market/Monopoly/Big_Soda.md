# [[Big_Soda]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Big Soda,4906383]]

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Big Soda** is a term used by the media and various activist groups 
> to describe the soft drink industry as a collective entity. 
> 
> The term connotes the business and lobbying power of soft drink companies who—
> like Big Tech, Big Oil, Big Pharma and Big Tobacco—
> would use that power to influence politicians and voters. 
> 
> One example is their lobbying against a tax on sugary drinks in the United States. 
> Big Soda forms a part of the broader sugar lobbying movement, Big Sugar.
>
> Big Soda usually refers to the giants of the soft drink industry, such as 
> The Coca-Cola Company, PepsiCo, and Keurig Dr Pepper.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big%20Soda)
