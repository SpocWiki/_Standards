## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A hoe is an ancient and versatile agricultural and horticultural hand tool 
> used to shape soil, remove weeds, clear soil, and harvest root crops. 
> 
> Shaping the soil includes piling soil around the base of plants (hilling), 
> digging narrow furrows (drills) and shallow trenches for planting seeds or bulbs. 
> 
> Weeding with a hoe includes agitating the surface of the soil or cutting foliage from roots, 
> and clearing the soil of old roots and crop residues. 
> Hoes for digging and moving soil are used to harvest root crops such as potatoes.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hoe%20(tool))
