## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Quarry** is a type of open-pit mine in which dimension stone, rock, 
> construction aggregate, riprap, sand, gravel, or slate is excavated from the ground. 
> 
> The operation of quarries is regulated in some jurisdictions 
> to manage their safety risks and reduce their environmental impact.
>
> The word quarry can also include the underground quarrying for stone, such as Bath stone.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quarry)
