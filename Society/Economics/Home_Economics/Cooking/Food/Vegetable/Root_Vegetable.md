## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Root vegetables are underground plant parts eaten by humans or animals as food. 
> 
> In agricultural and culinary terminology, the term applies to true roots, 
> such as taproots and root tubers, 
> as well as non-roots such as bulbs, corms, rhizomes, and stem tubers.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Root%20vegetable)
