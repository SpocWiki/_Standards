#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Samosa,491517]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Samosa** is a fried South Asian pastry with a savory filling 
> that mostly consists of vegetables like spiced potatoes, onions, and peas, 
> but can also include meat, fish, or  cheese. 
> 
> Its name originates from the Middle Persian word sambosag (سنبوسگ) 
> (meaning 'triangular pastry'). 
> 
> It is made in different shapes, including triangular, cone, or crescent, 
> depending on the region. 
> 
> Samosas are often accompanied by chutney, and have origins in medieval times or earlier. 
> Sweet versions are also made. 
> 
> Samosas are a popular entrée, appetizer, or snack in the cuisines of South Asia, 
> Southeast Asia, West Asia, Central Asia, Portugal, East Africa and their South Asian diasporas.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samosa)
