# [[Porridge]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Porridge,186817]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Porridge** is a food made by heating, soaking or boiling 
> ground, crushed or chopped starchy plants, typically grain, in milk or water. 
> 
> It is often cooked or served with added flavourings such as sugar, honey, fruit, or syrup 
> to make a sweet cereal, or it can be mixed with spices, meat, or vegetables to make a savoury dish. 
> 
> It is usually served hot in a bowl, depending on its consistency. 
> Oat porridge, known as oatmeal in North America, is one of the most common types. 
> Gruel is a thinner version of porridge, and congee is a savoury variation of porridge of Asian origin.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Porridge)
