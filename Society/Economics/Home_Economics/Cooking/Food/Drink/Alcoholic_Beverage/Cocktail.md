## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Cocktail** is a mixed drink, usually  alcoholic. 
> Most commonly, a cocktail is a combination of one or more spirits 
> mixed with other ingredients, such as juices, flavored syrups, tonic water, shrubs, and bitters. 
> Cocktails vary widely across regions of the world, 
> and many websites publish both original recipes 
> and their own interpretations of older and more famous cocktails.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cocktail)
