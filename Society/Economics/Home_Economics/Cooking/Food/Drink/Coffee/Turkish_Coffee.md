## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Turkish coffee is a style of coffee prepared in a cezve 
> using very finely ground coffee beans 
> without filtering or settling to remove the grounds.  
> 
> Similar beverages go under different names 
> in the Middle and Near East and eastern Europe.
>
