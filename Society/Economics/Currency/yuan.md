## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Renminbi** (Chinese: 人民币; pinyin: Rénmínbì; lit. 'People's Currency' Chinese 
> pronunciation: [ʐən˧˥min˧˥pi˥˩]; symbol: ¥; ISO code: CNY; abbreviation: RMB) 
> is the official currency of China. 
> 
> The renminbi is issued by the People's Bank of China, the monetary authority of China. 
> It is the world's fifth-most-traded currency as of April 2022. 
> The Chinese yuan (元) is the basic unit of the renminbi. 
>
> One yuan is divided into 10 jiao (角), and the jiao is further subdivided into 10 fen (分). 
> The word yuan is widely used to refer to the Chinese currency generally, 
> especially in international contexts.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Renminbi)
