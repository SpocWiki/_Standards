## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Moving the goalposts** (or shifting the goalposts) is a metaphor, 
> derived from goal-based sports such as football and hockey, 
> that means to __change the rule or criterion__ ("goal") of a process or competition 
> __while it is still in progress__, in such a way 
> that the new goal offers __one side an advantage__.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moving%20the%20goalposts)
