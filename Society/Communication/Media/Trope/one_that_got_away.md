# [[one_that_got_away]] 

The expression “the one that got away” refers to a person or opportunity perceived as uniquely valuable, lost through inaction, timing, or circumstances, and remembered with lasting regret or nostalgia.  
The phrase is idiomatic English, most commonly applied to romantic relationships, but also used for careers, investments, or life chances.
