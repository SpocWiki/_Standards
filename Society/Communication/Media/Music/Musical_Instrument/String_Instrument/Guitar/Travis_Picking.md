# [[Travis_Picking]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Travis Pickin'** is an instrumental acoustic solo album 
> by American country guitarist Merle Travis 
> released by the CMH Records label in 1981 as an LP recording. 
> 
> It was not released on compact disc, but was made available for download in 2018.  
> Travis was nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Country Instrumental Performance 
> for the album in 1981.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travis%20Pickin')
