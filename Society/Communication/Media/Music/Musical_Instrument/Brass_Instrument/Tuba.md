## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Tuba** is the largest and lowest-pitched musical instrument in the brass family. 
> 
> As with all brass instruments, the sound is produced by lip vibration – 
> a buzz – into a mouthpiece. 
> 
> It first appeared in the mid-19th century, 
> making it one of the newer instruments in the modern orchestra and concert band, 
> and largely replaced the ophicleide. 
> Tuba is Latin for "trumpet".
>
> A person who plays the tuba is called a tubaist, a tubist, or simply a tuba player. 
> In a British brass band or military band, they are known as bass players.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tuba)
