# [[Strauss,Johann_I]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Johann Baptist Strauss I (German: [ˈjoːhan bapˈtɪst ˈʃtʁaʊs]; 
> 14 March 1804 – 25 September 1849), 
> also known as Johann Strauss Sr., the Elder or the Father 
> (German: Johann Strauß Vater), 
> was an Austrian composer of the Romantic Period. 
> 
> He was famous for his light music, 
> namely waltzes, polkas, and galops, 
> which he popularized alongside Joseph Lanner, 
> thereby setting the foundations for his sons—
> Johann, Josef and Eduard—to carry on his musical dynasty. 
> 
> He is best known for his composition of the Radetzky March 
> (named after Joseph Radetzky von Radetz).
>
