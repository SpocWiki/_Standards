## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Edvard Hagerup Grieg ( GREEG, Norwegian: [ˈɛ̀dvɑʈ ˈhɑ̀ːɡərʉp ˈɡrɪgː]; 15 June 1843 – 4 September 1907) 
> was a Norwegian composer and pianist. 
> 
> He is widely considered one of the leading Romantic era composers, 
> and his music is part of the standard classical repertoire worldwide. 
> His use of Norwegian folk music in his own compositions brought the music of Norway to fame, 
> as well as helping to develop a national identity, 
> much as Jean Sibelius did in Finland and Bedřich Smetana in Bohemia.
>
> Grieg is the most celebrated person from the city of Bergen, 
> with numerous statues that depict his image and many cultural entities named after him: 
> the city's largest concert building (Grieg Hall), its most advanced music school (Grieg Academy) 
> and its professional choir (Edvard Grieg Kor). 
> The Edvard Grieg Museum at Grieg's former home, Troldhaugen, is dedicated to his legacy.
>
