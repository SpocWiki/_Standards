#has_/creator :: [[../System_of_a_Down|System_of_a_Down]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> "Chop Suey!" is a song by the American heavy metal band System of a Down. 
> It was released on August 13, 2001, as the first single from their second album, Toxicity (2001). 
> 
> The single earned the band its first Grammy nomination in 2002 
> for Best Metal Performance at the 44th Annual Grammy Awards. 
> 
> "Chop Suey!" is often considered the band's signature song. 
> Its music video has reached one billion views on YouTube.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chop%20Suey!)
