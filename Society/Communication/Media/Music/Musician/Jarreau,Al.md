## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Alwin Lopez Jarreau (March 12, 1940 – February 12, 2017) was an American singer and songwriter. 
> His 1981 album Breakin' Away spent two years on the Billboard 200 
> and is considered one of the finest examples of the Los Angeles pop and R&B sound. 
> 
> The album won Jarreau the 1982 Grammy for Best Male Pop Vocal Performance. 
> In all, he won ten Grammy Awards and was nominated 19 other times during his career.
>
> Jarreau also sang the theme song of the 1980s television series Moonlighting 
> and was among the performers on the 1985 charity song "We Are the World".
>
