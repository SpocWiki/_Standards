## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Vladimir Semyonovich Vysotsky (25 January 1938 – 25 July 1980) 
> was a Soviet singer-songwriter, poet, and actor 
> who had an immense and enduring effect on Soviet culture. 
> 
> He became widely known for his unique singing style 
> and for his lyrics, which featured social and political commentary 
> in often-humorous street jargon. 
> 
> He was also a prominent stage- and screen-actor. 
> Though the official Soviet cultural establishment largely ignored his work, 
> he was remarkably popular during his lifetime 
> and has exerted significant influence on many of Russia's musicians and actors.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vladimir%20Vysotsky)
