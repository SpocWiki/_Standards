## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Enrico Caruso (25 February 1873 – 2 August 1921) was an Italian operatic tenor, 
> who sang to great acclaim at the major opera houses of Europe and the Americas, 
> appearing in a wide variety of roles that ranged from the lyric to the dramatic. 
> 
> Generally recognized as the first international recording star, 
> Caruso made around 250 commercially released recordings from 1902 to 1920.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enrico%20Caruso)
