## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> "Both Sides, Now" is a song by Canadian singer-songwriter Joni Mitchell. 
> One of the first recordings is by Judy Collins, 
> whose version appeared on the US singles chart during the fall of 1968. 
> (The earliest commercial release was 
> by Dave Van Ronk and the Hudson Dusters, under the title "Clouds", 
> released in June 1967.) 
> 
> The next year it was included on Mitchell's album Clouds, 
> and became one of her best-known songs. 
> It has since been recorded by dozens of artists, 
> including Dion in 1968, Clannad with Paul Young in 1991, 
> and Mitchell herself, who re-recorded the song 
> with an orchestral arrangement on her 2000 album Both Sides Now.
> 
> In 2004, Rolling Stone ranked "Both Sides, Now" 
> at number 170 on its list of the 500 Greatest Songs.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Both%20Sides,%20Now)
