## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Reuters** (ROY-tÉ™rz) is a British news agency owned by Thomson Reuters. 
> It employs around 2,500 journalists and 600 photojournalists 
> in about 200 locations worldwide writing in 16 languages. 
> Reuters is one of the largest news agencies in the world.
>
> The agency was established in London in 1851 by the German baron Paul Reuter. The Thomson Corporation of Canada acquired the agency in a 2008 corporate merger, resulting in the formation of the Thomson Reuters Corporation.
>
> In December 2024, Reuters was ranked as the 27th most visited news site in the world, with over 105 million monthly readers.
>
