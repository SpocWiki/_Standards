## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Scientific American, informally abbreviated SciAm or sometimes SA, 
> is an American popular science magazine. 
> 
> Many scientists, including Albert Einstein and Nikola Tesla, have contributed articles to it, 
> with more than 150 Nobel Prize-winners having been featured since its inception.
>
> In print since 1845, it is the oldest continuously published magazine in the United States. 
> Scientific American is owned by Springer Nature, 
> which is a subsidiary of Holtzbrinck Publishing Group.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific%20American)
