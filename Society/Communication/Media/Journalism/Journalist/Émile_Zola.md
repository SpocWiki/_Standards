## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Émile Édouard Charles Antoine Zola (, also US: ; French: [emil zɔla]; 2 April 1840 – 29 September 1902) 
> was a French novelist, journalist, playwright, 
> the best-known practitioner of the literary school of naturalism, 
> and an important contributor to the development of theatrical naturalism. 
> 
> He was a major figure in the political liberalization of France 
> and in the exoneration of the falsely accused and convicted army officer Alfred Dreyfus, 
> which is encapsulated in his renowned newspaper opinion headlined J'Accuse...!  
> 
> Zola was nominated for the first and second Nobel Prizes in Literature in 1901 and 1902.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%89mile%20Zola)
