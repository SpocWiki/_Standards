## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Martin Suter** (born 29 February 1948 in Zürich) is a Swiss author. 
> He became known for his weekly column Business Class in the Weltwoche newspaper (1992–2004), 
> now appearing in the Tages-Anzeiger, and another column appearing in "NZZ Folio". 
> The columns have been published as nine books.
>
> Suter has published fourteen novels, written four stage plays, and seven screenplays, 
> for which he received various awards.
>
> He is married and lives in Spain and Guatemala.
>
> Suter worked previously as a copywriter and creative director in advertising 
> before he decided to fully concentrate on his writing career in 1991. 
> He achieved his break-through with his novel Small World in 1997, 
> which was published by Diogenes Verlag.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Martin%20Suter)
