## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Abu Nuwas** (أبو نواس, Abū Nuwās) (c. 756 – c. 814) was a classical Arabic poet, 
> and the foremost representative of the modern (muhdath) poetry 
> that developed during the first years of Abbasid Caliphate. 
> 
> He also entered the folkloric tradition, appearing several times in [[One Thousand and One Nights]].
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abu%20Nuwas)
