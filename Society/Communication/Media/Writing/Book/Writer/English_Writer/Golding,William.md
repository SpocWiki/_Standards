## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Sir William Gerald Golding  (19 September 1911 – 19 June 1993) 
> was a British novelist, playwright, and poet. 
> 
> Best known for his debut novel Lord of the Flies (1954), 
> Golding published another 12 volumes of fiction in his lifetime. 
> 
> In 1980, Golding was awarded the Booker Prize for Rites of Passage, 
> the first novel in what became his sea trilogy, To the Ends of the Earth. 
> He was awarded the 1983 Nobel Prize in Literature.
>
> As a result of his contributions to literature, Golding was knighted in 1988. 
> He was also a fellow of the Royal Society of Literature. 
> In 2008, The Times ranked Golding third on its list of "The 50 greatest British writers since 1945".
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William%20Golding)
