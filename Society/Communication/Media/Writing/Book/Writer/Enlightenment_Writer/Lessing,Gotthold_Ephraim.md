## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Gotthold Ephraim Lessing (; German: [ˈɡɔthɔlt ˈʔeːfʁa.ɪm ˈlɛsɪŋ] ; 22 January 1729 – 15 February 1781) 
> was a German philosopher, dramatist, publicist and art critic, 
> and a representative of the Enlightenment era. 
> 
> His plays and theoretical writings substantially influenced the development of German literature. 
> He is widely considered by theatre historians to be the first dramaturg in his role 
> at Abel Seyler's Hamburg National Theatre.
>
> The word Dramaturgy first appears in his work Hamburg Dramaturgy.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gotthold%20Ephraim%20Lessing)
