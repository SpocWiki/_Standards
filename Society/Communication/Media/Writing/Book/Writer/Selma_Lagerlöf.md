## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Selma Ottilia Lovisa Lagerlöf (, US also , Swedish: [ˈsɛ̂lːma ˈlɑ̂ːɡɛˌɭøːv] ; 
> 20 November 1858 – 16 March 1940) was a Swedish writer. 
> 
> She published her first novel, Gösta Berling's Saga, at the age of 33. 
> She was the first woman to win the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1909. 
> 
> In 1914, she was the first woman to be granted a membership of the Swedish Academy.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Selma%20Lagerl%C3%B6f)
