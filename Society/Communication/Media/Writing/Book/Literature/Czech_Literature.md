## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Czech literature can refer to literature written in Czech, in the Czech Republic 
> (formerly Czechoslovakia, earlier the Lands of the Bohemian Crown), or by Czech people.
>
> Most literature in the Czech Republic is now written in Czech, but historically, 
> a considerable part of Czech literary output was written in other languages as well, 
> including Latin and German.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Czech%20literature)
