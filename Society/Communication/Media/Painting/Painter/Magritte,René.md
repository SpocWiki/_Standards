## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> René François Ghislain Magritte (French: [ʁəne fʁɑ̃swa ɡilɛ̃ maɡʁit]; 
> 21 November 1898 – 15 August 1967) was a Belgian surrealist artist 
> known for his depictions of familiar objects in unfamiliar, unexpected contexts, 
> which often provoked questions about the nature and boundaries of reality and representation. 
> 
> His imagery has influenced pop art, minimalist art, and conceptual art.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ren%C3%A9%20Magritte)
