## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Joseph Louis Barrow (May 13, 1914 – April 12, 1981) 
> was an American professional boxer who competed from 1934 to 1951. 
> 
> Nicknamed "the Brown Bomber", 
> Louis is widely regarded as one of the greatest and most influential boxers of all time. 
> He reigned as the world heavyweight champion from 1937 until his temporary retirement in 1949. 
> He was victorious in 25 consecutive title defenses, a record for all weight classes. 
> Louis has the longest single reign as champion of any boxer in history.
>
> Louis's cultural impact was felt well outside the ring. 
> He is widely regarded as the first African-American 
> to achieve the status of a nationwide hero within the United States, 
> and was also a focal point of anti-Nazi sentiment leading up to and during World War II 
> because of his historic rematch with German boxer [[Schmeling,Max]] in 1938.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joe%20Louis)
