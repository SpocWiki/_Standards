# [[Roller_Sport]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~roller_sport,2164355]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Roller sport**s are sports that use human powered vehicles 
> which use rolling either by gravity or various pushing techniques. 
> 
> Typically ball bearings and polyurethane wheels 
> are used for momentum and traction respectively, 
> and are attached to devices or vehicles that the roller puts his weight on. 
> The international governing body is World Skate.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roller%20sports)
