## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Acrobatics** (from Ancient Greek  ἀκροβατέω (akrobatéō) 'walk on tiptoe, strut') 
> is the performance of human feats of balance, agility, and motor coordination. 
> 
> Acrobatic skills are used in performing arts, sporting events, and martial arts. Extensive use of acrobatic skills are most often performed in acro dance, circus, gymnastics, and freerunning and to a lesser extent in other athletic activities including ballet, slacklining and diving. Although acrobatics is most commonly associated with human body performance, the term is used to describe other types of performance, such as aerobatics.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acrobatics)
