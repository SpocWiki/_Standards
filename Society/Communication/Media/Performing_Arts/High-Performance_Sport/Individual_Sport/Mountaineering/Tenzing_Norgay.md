#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Tenzing_Norgay,80732|WD~Tenzing_Norgay,80732]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Tenzing Norgay (Sherpa: བསྟན་འཛིན་ནོར་རྒྱས tendzin norgyé; May 1914 – 9 May 1986), 
> born Namgyal Wangdi, and also referred to as Sherpa Tenzing, 
> was a Nepalese-Indian Sherpa mountaineer. 
> 
> On 29 May 1953, he and Edmund Hillary were the first confirmed to have 
> reached the summit of Mount Everest, as part of the 1953 British Mount Everest expedition. 
> 
> Time named Norgay one of the 100 most influential people of the 20th century.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tenzing%20Norgay)
