## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In many racing sports, an athlete (or occasionally a team of athletes) 
> will compete in a time trial (TT) against the clock to secure the fastest time. 
> 
> The format of a time trial can vary, but usually follow a format where 
> each athlete or team sets off at a predetermined interval to set the fastest time on a course.
>
