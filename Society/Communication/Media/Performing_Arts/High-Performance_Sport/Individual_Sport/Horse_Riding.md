## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Equestrianism** (from Latin equester, equestr-, equus, 'horseman', 'horse'), 
> commonly known as horse riding (Commonwealth English) or horseback riding (American English), 
> includes the disciplines of riding, driving, and vaulting. 
> 
> This broad description includes the use of horses for practical working purposes, 
> transportation, recreational activities, artistic or cultural exercises, and competitive sport.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equestrianism)
