## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Skiing** is the use of skis to glide on snow for basic transport, 
> a recreational activity, or a competitive winter sport. 
> 
> Many types of competitive skiing events 
> are recognized by the International Olympic Committee (IOC), 
> and the International Ski and Snowboard Federation (FIS).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skiing)
