# [[Waltz]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Waltz,193434|WD~Waltz,193434]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Waltz** (from German  Walzer [ˈvaltsɐ] , meaning "to roll or revolve") 
> is a ballroom and folk dance, in triple (3/4) time, performed primarily in closed position. 
> 
> Along with the ländler and allemande, the waltz was sometimes referred to 
> by the generic term German Dance in publications 
> during the late 18th and early 19th centuries.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Waltz)
