## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Tightrope walking, also called funambulism, is the skill of walking along a thin wire or rope. 
> It has a long tradition in various countries and is commonly associated with the circus. 
> 
> Other skills similar to tightrope walking include slack rope walking and slacklining.
>
