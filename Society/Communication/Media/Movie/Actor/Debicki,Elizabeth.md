## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Elizabeth Debicki** (born 24 August 1990) is an Australian actress. 
> Born in Paris and raised in Melbourne, she studied acting at the University of Melbourne, 
> and made her film debut in the comedy A Few Best Men (2011). 
> She gained wider recognition with her performance in Baz Luhrmann's period drama film 
> The Great Gatsby (2013), which won her the AACTA Award for Best Supporting Actress. 
>
> Debicki's profile grew with roles in the series Rake (2014), The Kettering Incident (2016) 
> and The Night Manager (2016), the HBO television film The Tale (2018), 
> and Steve McQueen's thriller film Widows (2018). 
> 
> In 2019, she received the Cannes Trophée Chopard. 
> Debicki received various accolades such as the Golden Globe and Primetime Emmy Award 
> for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series for portraying Diana, Princess of Wales 
> in the drama series The Crown (2022–2023). 
> 
> She has also played Ayesha in the Marvel Cinematic Universe films Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017) and Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 (2023). 
> Her other film credits include Christopher Nolan's science fiction Tenet (2020) 
> and Ti West's slasher MaXXXine (2024).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elizabeth%20Debicki)
