## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Marcel Reich-Ranicki (German: [maʁˈsɛl ˌʁaɪçʁaˈnɪtskiː]; 2 June 1920 – 18 September 2013) 
> was a Polish-born German literary critic and member of the informal literary association Gruppe 47. 
> He was regarded as one of the most influential contemporary literary critics 
> in the field of German literature 
> and has often been called Literaturpapst ("Pope of Literature") in Germany.
>
