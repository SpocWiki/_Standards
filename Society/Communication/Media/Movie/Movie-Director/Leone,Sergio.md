## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Sergio Leone** ( lee-OH-nee; Italian: [ˈsɛrdʒo leˈoːne]; 3 January 1929 – 30 April 1989) 
> was an Italian filmmaker, credited as the pioneer of the spaghetti Western genre. 
> He is widely regarded as one of the greatest directors in the history of cinema.
> 
> Leone's film-making style includes 
> juxtaposing extreme close-up shots with lengthy long shots. 
> 
> His films include the Dollars Trilogy of Westerns featuring Clint Eastwood: 
> - A Fistful of Dollars (1964), 
> - For a Few Dollars More (1965), and 
> - The Good, the Bad and the Ugly (1966); 
> and the Once Upon a Time films: 
> - Once Upon a Time in the West (1968), 
> - Duck, You Sucker! (1971), and 
> - Once Upon a Time in America (1984).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sergio%20Leone)
