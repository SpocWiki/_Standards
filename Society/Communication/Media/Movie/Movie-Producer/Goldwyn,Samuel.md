## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Samuel Goldwyn ( GOHLD-win; born Szmuel Gelbfisz; Yiddish: שמואל געלבפֿיש; 
> July 1879 – January 31, 1974) was a Polish-born American film producer 
> and pioneer in the American film industry, 
> who produced the United States's first major motion picture. 
> 
> He was best known for being the founding contributor 
> and executive of several motion picture studios. 
> 
> He was awarded the Golden Globe Cecil B. DeMille Award (1973), 
> the Irving G. Thalberg Memorial Award (1947), and the Jean Hersholt Humanitarian Award (1958).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel%20Goldwyn)
