#is_/same_as :: [[WD~The_Little_Mermaid,214619]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Little Mermaid (Danish: Den lille Havfrue) is a bronze statue by Edvard Eriksen, 
> depicting a mermaid becoming human. 
> The sculpture is displayed on a rock by the waterside at the Langelinie promenade 
> in Copenhagen, Denmark. 
> 
> It is 1.25 metres (4.1 ft) tall and weighs 175 kilograms (385 lb).
>
> Based on the 1837 fairy tale of the same name by Danish author Hans Christian Andersen, 
> the small and unimposing statue is a Copenhagen icon 
> and has been a major tourist attraction since its unveiling in 1913. 
> In recent decades it has become a popular target for defacement by vandals and political activists.
>
> Mermaid is among iconic statues that symbolize cities; others include: 
> - the statue of Pania of the Reef in Napier; 
> - Manneken Pis in Brussels; 
> - the Statue of Liberty in New York; 
> - Christ the Redeemer in Rio de Janeiro; 
> - Smok Wawelski (Wawel Dragon) in Kraków, Poland; or 
> - Nelson's Column and Eros in London.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Little%20Mermaid%20(statue))
