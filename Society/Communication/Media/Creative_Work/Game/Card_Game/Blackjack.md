## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Blackjack** (formerly black jack or vingt-un) is a casino banking game. 
> It is the most widely played casino banking game in the world. 
> It uses decks of 52 cards and descends from a global family of casino banking games known as "twenty-one". 
> This family of card games also includes the European games vingt-et-un and pontoon, 
> and the Russian game Ochko. 
> 
> The game is a comparing card game where players compete against the dealer, rather than each other.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blackjack)
