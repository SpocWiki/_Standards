# [[Suit~Spades]] ♠ 


#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Spades_(suit),2094951]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Spades (♠) (French: Pique) is one of the four playing 
> card suits in the standard French-suited playing cards. 
> 
> It has the same shape as the leaf symbol in German-suited playing cards 
> but its appearance is more akin to that of 
> an upside down black heart with a stalk at its base. 
> 
> It symbolises the pike or halberd, two medieval weapons, 
> but is actually an adaptation of the German suit symbol of Leaves 
> created when French suits were invented around 1480.
> 
> In bridge, spades rank as the highest suit. 
> In skat and similar games, it is the second-highest suit.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spades%20(suit))
