## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Lovers (VI) is the sixth trump or Major Arcana card 
> in most traditional Tarot decks. 
> It is used in game playing as well as in divination.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Lovers)  

## Meaning 

- Attraction, 
- love, 
- beauty, 
- trials overcome. 

## Reversed 

- Failure, 
- foolish designs. 
- Another account speaks of marriage frustrated 
  and contrarieties of all kinds
