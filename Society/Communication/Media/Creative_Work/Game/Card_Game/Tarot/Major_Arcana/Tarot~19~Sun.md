## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Sun (XIX) is the nineteenth trump or Major Arcana card in most traditional tarot decks. It is commonly associated with joy, success, vitality, and illumination. The card symbolizes positivity and represents a time of clarity and personal growth.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Sun%20(tarot%20card))  

## Meaning 

According to Waite it is said to reflect happiness and contentment, vitality, self-confidence, and success. 
The card is often seen as a sign of achievement and authentic self-expression. 

When drawn upright, The Sun signifies 
- success, 
- happiness, and 
- enlightenment. 
- It suggests a period where the truth is revealed, 
  leading to optimism and personal fulfillment. 
## drawn reversed 

- temporary setbacks
- self-doubt 
- delayed success 
- its overall positive nature suggests that 
	- any challenges are transient and 
	- can be overcome with persistence.
