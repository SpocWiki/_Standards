
# [[Tarot~15~Devil]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Devil (XV) is the fifteenth trump or Major Arcana card 
> in most traditional tarot decks. 
> 
> It is used in game playing as well as in divination.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Devil%20(tarot%20card))  

## Meaning 

that which is predestined but is not for this reason evil. 
- Ravage, 
- violence, 
- vehemence, 
- extraordinary efforts, 
- force, 
- fatality

## Reversed 

- Evil fatality, 
- weakness, 
- pettiness, 
- blindness
