## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Chariot (VII) is the seventh trump 
> or Major Arcana card in most traditional tarot decks. 
> 
> It is used in game playing as well as in divination.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Chariot%20(tarot%20card))  

## Meaning 

- Succour, 
- providence; 
- also war, 
- triumph, 
- presumption, 
- vengeance, 
- trouble. 

## Reversed: 

- Riot, 
- quarrel, 
- dispute, 
- litigation, 
- defeat
