
# [[Tarot~17~Star]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Star (XVII) is the 17th ranking or Major Arcana card 
> in most traditional tarot decks. 
> 
> It is used in game playing as well as in divination.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Star%20(tarot%20card))  

## Meaning 

- Loss, 
- theft, 
- privation, 
- abandonment; 
- another reading says-Hope and bright prospects

## Reversed 

- arrogance, 
- haughtiness, 
- impotence
