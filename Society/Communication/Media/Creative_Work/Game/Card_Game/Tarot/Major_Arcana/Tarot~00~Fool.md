## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Fool is one of the 78 cards in a tarot deck. 
> 
> Traditionally, it is the lowest of the 22 trump cards, 
> in tarot card reading called the 22 Major Arcana. 
> 
> However, in tarot card games it developed to be 
> not one of the (then 21) trump cards but a special card, 
> serving a unique purpose by itself. 
> 
> In later Central European tarot card games, 
> it re-developed to now become the highest trump. 
> 
> As a consequence and with respect to his unique history, 
> The Fool is usually an unnumbered card with a unique design; 
> but sometimes it is numbered as 0 (the first) or more rarely XXII (the last). 
> 
> Design and numbering-or-not 
> clearly indicate its role as a trump or special card in the specific game.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Fool%20(tarot%20card))
