## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Wheel of Fortune is one of 78 cards in a tarot deck 
> and is the tenth trump or Major Arcana card in most tarot decks. 
> 
> It is used in game playing as well as in divination.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wheel%20of%20Fortune%20(tarot%20card))  

## Meaning 

- Destiny, 
- fortune, 
- success, 
- elevation, 
- luck, 
- felicity. 

## Reversed 

- Increase, 
- abundance, 
- superfluity
