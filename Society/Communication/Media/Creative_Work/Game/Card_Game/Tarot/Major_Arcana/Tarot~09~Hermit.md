## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Hermit (IX) is the ninth trump or Major Arcana card in most traditional tarot decks. It is used in game playing as well as in divination.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Hermit%20(tarot%20card))  

## Meaning 

- Prudence, 
- circumspection; 
- also and especially treason, 
- dissimulation, 
- roguery, 
- corruption. 

## Reversed 

- Concealment, 
- disguise, 
- policy, 
- fear, 
- unreasoned caution
