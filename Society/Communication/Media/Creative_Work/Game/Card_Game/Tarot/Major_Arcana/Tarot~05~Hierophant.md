## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Hierophant (V), alternatively depicted as The Pope or The High Priest 
> (as a counterpart to "The High Priestess") 
> is the fifth card of the Major Arcana 
> in occult Tarot decks used in divination. 
> 
> It was identified as the Pope in early decks like Tarot of Marseilles, 
> while modern decks like Rider–Waite Tarot may use the term hierophant 
> (Ancient Greek: ἱεροφάντης), a person who brings religious congregants 
> into the presence of that which is deemed "holy".
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20Hierophant)  

## Meaning 

- Marriage, 
- alliance, 
- captivity, 
- servitude; 
- by another account, 
- mercy, 
- and goodness; 
- inspiration; 
- the man to whom the Querent has recourse. 

## Reversed: 

- Society, good understanding, concord, over kindness, weakness
