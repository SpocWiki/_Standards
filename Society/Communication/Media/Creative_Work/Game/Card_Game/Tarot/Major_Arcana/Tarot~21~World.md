## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The World (XXI) is the 21st trump or Major Arcana card in the tarot deck. 
> It can be incorporated as the final card of the Major Arcana or 
> tarot trump sequence (the first or last optioned as being "The Fool" (0)). 
> 
> It is associated with the 22nd letter of the Hebrew alphabet, 'taw', 
> also spelled 'tav' or 'Taf'.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The%20World%20(tarot%20card))  


## Meaning 

- Assured success, 
- recompense, 
- voyage, 
- route, 
- emigration, 
- flight, 
- change of place. 

## Reversed

- Inertia, 
- fixity, 
- stagnation, 
- permanence
