
# [[Tarot~11~Justice]] 


## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Strength is a Major Arcana tarot card, and is numbered either XI or VIII, depending on the deck. 
> 
> Historically it was called Fortitude, 
> and in the Thoth Tarot deck it is called Lust. 
> 
> This card is used in game playing as well as in divination.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strength%20(tarot%20card))  

## Meaning 

- Power, 
- energy, 
- action, 
- courage, 
- magnanimity; 
- also complete success and honours. 

## Reversed 

- Despotism, 
- abuse of power, 
- weakness, 
- discord, 
- sometimes even disgrace
