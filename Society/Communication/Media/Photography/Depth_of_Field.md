## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The depth of field (DOF) is the distance between the nearest and the farthest objects 
> that are in acceptably sharp focus in an image captured with a camera. 
> See also the closely related depth of focus.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Depth%20of%20field)
