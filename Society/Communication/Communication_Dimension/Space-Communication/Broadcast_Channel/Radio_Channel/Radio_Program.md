## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Radio program**, radio programme, or radio show is a segment of content 
> intended for broadcast on radio. 
> 
> It may be a one-time production, or part of a periodically recurring series. 
> A single program in a series is called an episode.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Radio%20program)
