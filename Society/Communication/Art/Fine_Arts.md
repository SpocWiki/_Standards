# [[Fine_Arts]] 

#is_/different_from :: [[Decorative_Art]]  
#is_/same_as :: [[Fine_Arts,]] 

Fine Arts refers specifically to disciplines that are pursued primarily for their **aesthetic value** rather than functional utility:
- painting, 
- sculpture, 
- architecture, 
- music, 
- dance, 
- theater, and 
- poetry
