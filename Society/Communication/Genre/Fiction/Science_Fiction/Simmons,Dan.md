## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Dan Simmons** (April 4, 1948 – February 21, 2026) 
> was an American science fiction and horror writer. 
> 
> He was the author of the Hyperion Cantos and the Ilium/Olympos cycles, 
> among other works that span the science fiction, horror, and fantasy genres, 
> sometimes within a single novel. 
> 
> Simmons's genre-intermingling Song of Kali (1985) won the World Fantasy Award. 
> 
> He also wrote mysteries and thrillers, 
> some of which feature the continuing character Joe Kurtz.
>
