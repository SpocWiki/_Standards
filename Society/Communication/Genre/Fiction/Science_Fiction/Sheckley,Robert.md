## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Robert Sheckley** (July 16, 1928 – December 9, 2005) was an American writer. 
> First published in the science-fiction magazines of the 1950s, 
> his many quick-witted stories and novels were 
> famously unpredictable, absurdist, and broadly comical.
> 
> Nominated for Hugo and Nebula Awards, Sheckley was named Author Emeritus 
> by the Science Fiction and Fantasy Writers of America in 2001.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert%20Sheckley)
