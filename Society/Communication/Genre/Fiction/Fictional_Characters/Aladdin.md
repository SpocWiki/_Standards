# [[Aladdin]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../WikiData/WD~Aladdin,15605357]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Aladdin** ( ə-LAD-in; Arabic: علاء الدين, romanized: ʻAlāʼu d-Dīn/ʻAlāʼ ad-Dīn, IPA: [ʕalaːʔ adˈdiːn], ATU 561, 'Aladdin') is a Middle-Eastern folk tale. 
> 
> It is one of the best-known tales associated with One Thousand and One Nights 
> (often known in English as The Arabian Nights), despite not being part of the original text; 
> it was added by the Frenchman Antoine Galland, 
> based on a folk tale that he heard from the Syrian storyteller Hanna Diyab.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aladdin)
