## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Sociology of the family is a subfield of sociology 
> in which researchers and academics study family structure as a social institution 
> and unit of socialization from various sociological perspectives. 
> 
> It can be seen as an example of patterned social relations and group dynamics.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sociology%20of%20the%20family)
