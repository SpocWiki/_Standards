## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In economics, income distribution covers 
> how a country's total GDP is distributed amongst its population. 
> 
> Economic theory and economic policy have long seen 
> income and its distribution as a central concern. 
> 
> Unequal distribution of income causes economic inequality 
> which is a concern in almost all countries around the world.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Income%20distribution)
