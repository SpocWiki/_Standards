## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Structuralism** is an intellectual current and methodological approach, 
> primarily in the social sciences, that interprets elements of human culture 
> by way of their relationship to a broader system. 
> 
> It works to uncover the structural patterns that underlie all things that humans do, 
> think, perceive, and feel. 
>
> Alternatively, as summarized by philosopher Simon Blackburn, structuralism is: 
> "The belief that phenomena of human life are not intelligible 
> except through their interrelations. 
> 
> These relations constitute a structure, 
> and behind local variations in the surface phenomena 
> there are constant laws of abstract structure."
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Structuralism)
