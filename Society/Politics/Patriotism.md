## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Patriotism** is the feeling of love, devotion, and a sense of attachment to one's country or state. 
> 
> This attachment can be a combination of different feelings for things such as 
> the language of one's homeland, and its ethnic, cultural, political, or historical aspects. 
> 
> It may encompass a set of concepts closely related to nationalism, 
> mostly civic nationalism and sometimes cultural nationalism.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patriotism)
