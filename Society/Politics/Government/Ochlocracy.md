#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Ochlocracy,191031]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Mob rule or **Ochlocracy** or mobocracy is a pejorative term 
> describing an oppressive majoritarian form of government 
> controlled by the common people through the intimidation of authorities. 
> 
> Ochlocracy is distinguished from democracy 
> or similarly legitimate and representative governments 
> by the absence or impairment of a procedurally civil process reflective of the entire polity.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mob%20rule)
