# [[Triumvirate]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Triumvirate,83204]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Triumvirate** (Latin: triumvirātus) or a triarchy is a political institution 
> ruled or dominated by three individuals, known as triumvirs (Latin: triumviri). 
> 
> The arrangement can be formal or informal. 
> Though the three leaders in a triumvirate are notionally equal, 
> the actual distribution of power may vary. 
>
> Informally, the term "triumvirate" may be used for any association of three.
>
> Under the influence of the Soviet Union, 
> the term troika (Russian: for "group of three") may be used for "triumvirate".
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Triumvirate)
