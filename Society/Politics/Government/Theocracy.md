## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Theocracy** or ethiocracy is a form of autocracy or oligarchy 
> in which one or more deities are recognized as supreme ruling authorities, 
> giving divine guidance to human intermediaries, with executive, legislative, and/or judicial power, 
> who manage the government's daily affairs.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theocracy)
