

Below is a structured list of **independent political dimensions**, each with **disjoint (mutually exclusive) values within the dimension**.  
The dimensions themselves are conceptually independent (you can mix one value from each).

## 1) Economic Coordination

How an economy is organized.

- **Free Market (Laissez-Faire)** – Minimal state intervention
    
- **Regulated Market** – Market system with significant regulation
    
- **Mixed Economy** – Market + substantial public sector
    
- **State-Planned Economy** – Central economic planning
    
- **Communal/Decentralized Planning** – Cooperative or local planning without central state dominance

### ✅ Notes on This Hypercube

- **Each axis is independent:** You can mix any combination.
    
- **Disjoint values:** No two values on the same axis overlap.
    
- **Vector representation:** Each ideology = 36-dimensional vector.
    
- **Derivable axes:** 36 axes cover almost all policy domains; derivative axes (e.g., civil liberties + privacy) can be computed as functions of base axes.
