## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> In court proceedings, a **Defendant** 
> is a person or party either accused of committing a crime in criminal prosecution 
> or against whom some type of civil relief is being sought in a civil case.
>
> Terminology varies from one jurisdiction to another. 
> In Scots law, the terms "accused" or "panel" are used instead 
> in criminal proceedings and "defender" in civil proceedings. 
> Another term in use is "respondent".
>
