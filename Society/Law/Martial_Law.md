## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Martial law is the **replacement of civilian government by military rule** 
> **and the suspension of civilian legal processes for military powers**. 
> 
> Martial law can continue for a specified amount of time, or indefinitely, 
> and standard civil liberties may be suspended for as long as martial law continues. 
> 
> Most often, martial law is declared in times of war or emergencies 
> such as civil unrest and natural disasters. 
> 
> Alternatively, martial law may be declared in instances of military coups d'état.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Martial%20law)
