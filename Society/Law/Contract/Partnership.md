#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Partnership,728646]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> A **Partnership** is an agreement where parties agree to cooperate to advance their mutual interests. 
> The partners in a partnership may be individuals, businesses, 
> interest-based organizations, schools, governments or combinations. 
> 
> Organizations may partner to increase the likelihood of each achieving their mission 
> and to amplify their reach. 
> 
> A partnership may result in issuing and holding equity or may be only governed by a contract.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Partnership)
