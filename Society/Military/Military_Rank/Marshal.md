## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Marshal** is a term used in several official titles in various branches of society. 
> As marshals became trusted members of the courts of Medieval Europe, the title grew in reputation. 
> During the last few centuries, it has been used for elevated offices, 
> such as in military rank and civilian law enforcement.
>
> In most countries, the rank of Marshal is the highest Army rank 
> (equivalent to a five-star General of the Army in the United States).
>
