## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> An officer is a person who holds a position of authority 
> as a member of an armed force or uniformed service.
>
> Broadly speaking, "officer" means 
> - a commissioned officer, 
> - a non-commissioned officer (NCO), or a 
> - warrant officer. 
> 
> However, absent contextual qualification, the term typically refers 
> only to a force's commissioned officers, 
> the more senior members who derive their authority from a commission from the head of state.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Officer%20(armed%20forces))
