## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Desertion** is the abandonment of a military duty or post without permission 
> (a pass, liberty or leave) and is done with the intention of not returning. 
> 
> This contrasts with unauthorized absence (UA) or absence without leave (AWOL), 
> which are temporary forms of absence.
>
