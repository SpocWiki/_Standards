## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Colonel** (  KUR-nəl; abbreviated as Col., Col, or COL) 
> is a senior military officer rank used in many countries. 
> It is also used in some police forces and paramilitary organizations.
>
> In the 17th, 18th, and 19th centuries, a colonel was typically in charge of a regiment in an army. 
> Modern usage varies greatly, and in some cases, the term is used as an honorific title 
> that may have no direct military relationship. 
> 
> In some smaller military forces, such as those of Monaco or the Vatican, colonel is the highest rank.
>
> Equivalent naval ranks may be called captain or ship-of-the-line captain. 
> In the Commonwealth's air force ranking system, the equivalent rank is group captain.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colonel)
