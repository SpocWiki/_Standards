# [[Sabotage]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../WikiData/WD~Sabotage,193518|WD~Sabotage,193518]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Sabotage** is a deliberate action 
> aimed at weakening a polity, government, effort, or organization through 
> subversion, obstruction, demoralization,  destabilization, division, disruption, or destruction. 
> 
> One who engages in sabotage is a saboteur. 
> 
> Saboteurs typically try to conceal their identities 
> because of the consequences of their actions 
> and to avoid invoking legal and organizational requirements for addressing sabotage.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sabotage)
